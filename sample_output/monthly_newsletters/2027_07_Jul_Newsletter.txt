 MONTHLY SUMMARY
The month of July has proven to be a successful one for the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with 29 graduates and an active student count of 29. Our tire-changing experts have demonstrated their skills in our courses, leading to a sense of camaraderie and enthusiasm within the school's field.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Among this month's graduates, we are particularly proud of Leila Mehdi, Alaa El-Khatib, Juan Garcia, and Lena Park who received unit awards in recognition of their exceptional performance in Tyre Change Basics and Advanced Tyre Swap Techniques. Their hard work and dedication are a shining example for our current students and future cohorts.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into August, the focus will be on maintaining our high standards while welcoming two new cohorts to fill our school's 20 available seats. The challenges lie in ensuring that each student receives personalized attention to help them reach their full potential as tire-changing specialists. We look forward to another productive month at the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing.
