 MONTHLY SUMMARY
May has been a productive and exhilarating month at Helsinki School of Tyre Changing. With 65 graduates, we are witnessing an impressive wave of talent joining the automotive industry. The school's capacity has been fully utilized, which is both a testament to our strong curriculum and the high demand for skilled tyre changers in the field.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Among the graduates this month were Xiaoqing Zhang, Hassan Khan, Julian Nguyen, Ali Patel, Taro Mizuno, Omar Akbar, Yara Al-Jaber, Joaquin Gonzales, Hannah Ahmed, Ivan Petrov, Sofia Olsen, Leyla Sarioglu, and Sofia Lopez-Gonzalez. Each one of them has shown remarkable skill and dedication throughout their time at our school, and we eagerly await their contributions to the world of tyre changing.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into June, we anticipate another busy month with 4 new cohorts joining us. To ensure a smooth transition for these students, we will be focusing on enhancing our support systems, providing additional resources, and fostering a collaborative learning environment to help them excel in their studies. We welcome the challenges ahead and are excited to see more lives transformed through the art of tyre changing.
