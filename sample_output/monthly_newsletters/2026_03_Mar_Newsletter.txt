 MONTHLY SUMMARY

This past month has been a testament to our school's commitment and the dedication of our students. With 30 active learners, we saw impressive progress across various courses, particularly in Tyre Change Basics where 9 students received unit awards. As we approach spring and the bustling tyre-changing season, we are more optimistic than ever about our school's role in fostering the next generation of tyre changing professionals.

LOOKING AHEAD

As we move into April, we will continue to focus on expanding our course offerings, including Advanced Tyre Swap Techniques and Tyre Mounting and Dismounting Skills. We encourage new students to join us in mastering the art of tyre changing while current students should prepare for even more challenges and opportunities ahead. Together, we'll ensure that Helsinki School of Tyre Changing remains a leader in tire education and service in the field.
