 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
October was a remarkable month for the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing. With all 29 active students graduating and no dropouts, we've maintained our high standards and expanded our alumni network. The school's vibrant energy continues to fuel our passion for tire changing excellence in the dynamic field of automotive maintenance.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we celebrate the successes of three outstanding graduates: Zeynep Soylu, Ezra Tanaka, and Sebastián García. Their dedication, skill, and commitment to tire changing excellence have set them apart in our programs. We wish them continued success in their careers.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into November, we will welcome two new cohorts, increasing our student body to 41. With the added capacity, we look forward to continuing our mission of providing comprehensive tire changing education and fostering a community of passionate professionals. However, this growth brings challenges as well. We must ensure that our resources and facilities are prepared to accommodate the expanding school. Let's embrace these opportunities and face the challenges ahead together.
