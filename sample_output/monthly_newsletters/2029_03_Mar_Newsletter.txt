 MONTHLY SUMMARY
March proved to be a productive and successful month for the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing. We welcomed 39 active students, witnessed the graduation of two talented individuals – Theresa Ng and Aksel Nielsen – and saw 12 students receive unit awards in recognition of their hard work and dedication. With a total of 63 graduates this month, our tire-changing skills are being spread far and wide across the field.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we congratulate Theresa Ng for her remarkable achievement in mastering the art of tyre changing. Theresa's meticulous approach to learning and passion for excellence have set a high bar for future students at our school. Meanwhile, Aksel Nielsen displayed exceptional prowess in advanced tyre swap techniques, demonstrating that he is truly a rising star in our field.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into April, we aim to maintain the momentum built this month and continue nurturing the next generation of skilled tyre changers. We encourage all students to remain focused, dedicated, and always strive for perfection in their craft. Let us continue to push the boundaries of what is possible within our field and uphold the reputation of the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing.
