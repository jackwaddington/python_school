 MONTHLY SUMMARY
We have witnessed a productive June with a significant increase in graduates, reflecting the dedication and hard work of our students. The school's capacity has been fully utilized, but this is a testament to the growing demand for skilled tyre technicians in the automotive industry. Our field labs continue to provide an ideal environment for hands-on learning and practical application.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Fourty-five students graduated this month, demonstrating mastery of advanced tyre swapping techniques, mounting and dismounting skills. Congratulations to Aisha Al-Khateeb, Jonas Hansen, Sofia Gonzales, Aiko Yamada, Aksel Nielsen, Ana Santos, and Osman Khan on their achievements in Tyre Change Basics.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into July, we anticipate another active month with three new cohorts joining our school. The increased student numbers will present challenges in terms of resources and space, but we are confident that our dedicated team and commitment to quality education will ensure continued success. We encourage all students to embrace the learning journey ahead and to continue to push the boundaries of excellence in tyre changing.
