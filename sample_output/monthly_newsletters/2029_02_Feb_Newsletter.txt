 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This February has been a productive month for Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with a total of 63 graduates, an impressive increase in numbers compared to previous months. The school's dynamic atmosphere and dedicated students have contributed to this success. As we approach the peak tire-changing season, our focus is on maintaining this momentum while ensuring each student receives the best possible education in their chosen field.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Aisha Patel (she/her) from our Advanced Tyre Swap Techniques course received a unit award this month for her exceptional skills and dedication to excellence. Aisha's accomplishment serves as an inspiration to all current students and future cohorts.

LOOKING AHEAD
With only 16 seats available in our school, we look forward to next month's enrolment, particularly in the Tyre Change Basics course, where we hope to see more passionate individuals join our community. The increased demand for qualified tyre changers in the field calls for us to remain diligent in providing top-tier education and preparing our students for success.
