 MONTHLY SUMMARY
September has been a significant month for the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with 82 graduates and a total of 4 cohorts. This strong performance underscores our commitment to delivering quality education in the ever-evolving field of automotive maintenance. Our school capacity remains at 16 seats, ensuring we can continue to train dedicated students in the essential skills of tyre changing.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to Amina Diop, Lukas Sakai, Leonardo Gomez, Aisha Patel, Aina Jakobsdotter, Raman Sharma, Taro Mizuno, Xiaoqing Zhang, Ivan Petrov, Hassan Khan, Hana Murakami, Hana Al-Khalili, Omar Akbar, and Hannah Ahmed who graduated this month. Their dedication and hard work in the Tyre Mounting and Dismounting Skills course are a testament to their passion for the industry.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into October, our focus remains on maintaining our high standards of education while also addressing the recent rise in dropouts. We will be implementing additional support systems to ensure students receive the assistance they need to succeed and complete their programs. We look forward to another productive month at Helsinki School of Tyre Changing.
