June has been a successful month for our school, with a total of 27 active students and no dropouts. The enthusiasm and dedication displayed by our learners have been remarkable. With three graduates, we are proud to see them embark on their new careers in the dynamic tyre industry. Our school's field provides a real-world context that makes learning engaging and relevant for our students.
