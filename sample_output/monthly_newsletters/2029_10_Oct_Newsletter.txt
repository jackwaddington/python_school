 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
October has been a fruitful month for the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with an impressive 85 graduates and 14 active students currently enrolled. Despite the 21 dropouts, we are proud to have maintained our school capacity of 16 seats. Our tire technicians have demonstrated resilience and skill in navigating various vehicle types on both asphalt and gravel fields, showcasing our commitment to excellence in the field.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to Felipe Gonzalez, Yuna Nakano, and Amina Ben-Abdallah for their achievements this month! Felipe and Yuna excelled in Advanced Tyre Swap Techniques, while Amina demonstrated her proficiency in the same unit. We are proud of your tire changing prowess and wish you all continued success in your future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we look forward to November, our focus will be on maintaining a strong student-to-teacher ratio, ensuring that each student receives personalized attention and guidance. We expect another productive month of learning and growth as we continue to shape the next generation of tire changing professionals. Keep up the great work!
