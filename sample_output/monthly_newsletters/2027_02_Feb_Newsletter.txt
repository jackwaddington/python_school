February has been a highly productive month for the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing. With 29 graduates, our numbers remain impressive. The school's tire-changing field continues to serve as an invaluable hands-on learning environment, fostering students' practical skills and confidence.

As we move into March, our focus is on maintaining high enrollment numbers, continuing to provide quality education, and ensuring a smooth transition for our new cohorts. The demand for skilled tire changers remains strong, so it's crucial that we maintain our commitment to excellence in teaching and student success.
