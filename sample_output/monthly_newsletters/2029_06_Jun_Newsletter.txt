 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
June was a productive and engaging month at the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing. With 68 graduates, our team is growing stronger in tyre changing skills and expertise. The school's capacity remains well utilized, with an active student body of 34 learners. As we approach summer, let us remember to keep our focus on the road ahead – where safety and precision are key.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to Nina Nguyen, Sami Korhonen, Osman Khan, Amina Omer, and Sara Karttunen, who have successfully completed their courses this month. Their dedication and mastery of advanced tyre swap techniques and tyre mounting and dismounting skills will undoubtedly make a positive impact on our community's roads.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we enter the summer season, we anticipate increased demand for tyre change services. With this in mind, our upcoming July courses will focus on reinforcing essential skills to ensure that all graduates are well-prepared and confident when handling various vehicle types. Let us continue to strive for excellence and uphold the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing's reputation as a leading institution in tyre changing education.
