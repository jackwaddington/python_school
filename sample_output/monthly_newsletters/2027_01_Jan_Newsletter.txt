 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
January was a productive and successful month for Helsinki School of Tyre Changing. With 33 active students, we have exceeded our school capacity of 20 seats, demonstrating the demand for our education in the field. This month, we graduated an impressive 26 students, signifying a strong start to the year. Unfortunately, we also had one dropout, a reminder that every student's journey is unique and not without challenges.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our graduates, including Anna Lehto and Mikko Kauppinen, who demonstrated exceptional skills in Tyre Change Basics and Tyre Mounting and Dismounting Skills respectively. Their dedication and hard work are an inspiration to all of us at the school.

LOOKING AHEAD:
February will be a crucial month as we aim to accommodate more students while maintaining our high standards of education. We anticipate adding more courses, such as Advanced Tyre Swap Techniques, to cater to the growing demand in the industry. We encourage new and returning students to embrace this opportunity to further their skills and careers in tyre changing.
