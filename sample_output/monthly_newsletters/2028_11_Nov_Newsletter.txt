 MONTHLY SUMMARY
November has been a productive month at Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with 14 active students diligently mastering the art of tyre changing. We graduated an impressive 59 students, marking our highest number of graduates in one month since our establishment. Although we experienced a few dropouts, our dedicated students continue to demonstrate their passion for the field and commitment to excelling in tyre change techniques.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, Osman Khan, Amina Omer, and Aksel Nielsen were recognized with unit awards for their exceptional achievements in Advanced Tyre Swap Techniques. Each of these students has shown remarkable skill and dedication to the field, setting an inspiring example for our current students.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we enter December, our focus will be on maintaining a high standard of education while ensuring that our school remains accessible to those eager to learn. We anticipate increased enrollment in our courses, particularly Tyre Mounting and Dismounting Skills. To accommodate this growth, we will continue to work on expanding our capacity to serve even more students in the coming months.
