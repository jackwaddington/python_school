 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
October has been a fruitful month for Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with 59 graduates adding to our growing community of skilled tyre technicians. This marks the highest number of graduates in one month since the school's establishment. The energy and enthusiasm from our students have been truly inspiring. Our field is thriving as more individuals embrace the art and science of tyre changing.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Yara El-Masri, a dedicated student, demonstrated exceptional skill in advanced tyre swapping techniques during this month's Unit Award Ceremony. Yara's commitment to mastering the craft has earned her the respect and admiration of fellow students and staff alike. We wish Yara all the best in their future endeavors!

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into November, we anticipate a challenging but rewarding month ahead. With 16 seats available, we invite anyone eager to learn and contribute to our community to enroll in our courses. Let's continue growing and fostering excellence in the field of tyre changing!
