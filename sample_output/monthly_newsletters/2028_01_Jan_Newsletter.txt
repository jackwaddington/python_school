 MONTHLY SUMMARY
January was a successful and productive month for the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with 35 graduates and only 5 dropouts. The active student count currently stands at 50, demonstrating our growing influence in the field of automotive service education. We are proud to see our students excelling in their studies, particularly in courses such as Advanced Tyre Swap Techniques.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to Sophie Lee, Lena Park, Felipe Gonzalez, Leila Mehdi, Alaa El-Khatib, and Ezra Tanaka for their recent graduation from our school. Their dedication and hard work have resulted in the mastery of essential tyre changing skills that will serve them well in the industry. We wish them all continued success!

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into February, we aim to maintain our high standards while welcoming new students to fill our 20 available seats across three cohorts. Our goal for next month is to ensure every student receives the personalized attention and education they deserve in order to excel in their chosen field. We look forward to another productive and rewarding month ahead!
