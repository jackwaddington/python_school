Q1: What method allows a vehicle to continue driving safely after a puncture in a run-flat tire without an external air source?
  A) Tire Mounting Machine Operation
  B) TPMS Recalibration
  C) Wheel Balancing Techniques
  D) Run-Flat Tire Changeover Technique
Answer: D

Q2: Which step is necessary when replacing one or more tires on a vehicle equipped with TPMS?
  A) Mounting the new tire onto the wheel
  B) Using a diagnostic tool to program the new tire sensors
  C) Inflating the new tire to its recommended pressure
  D) Removing the old tire from the hub
Answer: B
