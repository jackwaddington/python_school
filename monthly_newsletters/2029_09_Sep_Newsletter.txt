 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Monthly Newsletter - September 2029

Dear Community Members,

We are pleased to present the latest update from Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits for the month of September 2029.

Current Enrollment: 75 active students
Graduates this Month: 120
Dropouts this Month: 55
Total Cohorts enrolled: 5

We are proud to announce the successful graduation of 120 students this month, adding to our distinguished alumni. We bid farewell and wish them the best in their future endeavors. We also acknowledge that 55 students have decided to discontinue their studies with us. Our counselors will be reaching out to them individually for feedback and assistance.

We welcome five new cohorts into our learning community this month, bringing our total enrollment to 75 active students. We are excited about the diverse backgrounds, interests, and potential each of these students brings to our academy.

As we embark on a new academic year, we remain committed to providing an engaging, challenging, and nurturing environment for all our students. Our faculty continues to strive towards excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement.

Stay tuned for more updates as the academic year progresses. We look forward to another year of intellectual pursuits, growth, and success at Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits.
