Monthly Newsletter - August 2028

Dear Parents and Guardians,

We are pleased to share the latest updates from Empyrean Academy for the month of August. This newsletter aims to provide you with a comprehensive overview of our ongoing activities and achievements.

As we conclude the academic year, we celebrate the successes of our 71 graduating students, marking a total of 96 active students currently enrolled. Unfortunately, we also bid farewell to 33 students who have decided to pursue other paths.

We welcome four new cohorts into our academic community this month. We look forward to their contributions and the unique perspectives they bring to Empyrean Academy.

Our dedicated faculty and staff remain committed to fostering an environment that encourages intellectual growth, critical thinking, and personal development. We are excited about the upcoming events and initiatives planned for the new academic year.

We appreciate your continued support and partnership in helping us shape the future leaders of tomorrow.

Stay informed by visiting our website at www.empyreanacademy.org or following us on social media for regular updates.

Warm Regards,
Empyrean Academy Administration
