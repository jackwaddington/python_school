 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Monthly Newsletter
February 2028

Dear Parents and Guardians,

We are pleased to present our February newsletter featuring the latest updates from Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits.

Current Enrollment: As of February 21st, we have 52 active students in our school.

Graduates: A total of 69 students graduated this month, marking a significant achievement for both the graduates and Empyrean Academy.

Dropouts: Regrettably, we bid farewell to 29 students who chose to leave our institution. We wish them all the best in their future endeavors.

Cohorts Enrolled: This month saw the addition of three new cohorts to our academic community. We extend a warm welcome to these new students and look forward to their contributions to Empyrean Academy.

We continue to strive for excellence in education and remain committed to fostering an environment conducive to learning, growth, and development.

Sincerely,
The Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Team
