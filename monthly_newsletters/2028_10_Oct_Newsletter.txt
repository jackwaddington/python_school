Dear Parents and Guardians,

We are pleased to present the October progress report for our esteemed institution, Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits.

Current Enrollment: 91 active students
Graduates for October: 74
Dropouts for October: 35
Total Cohorts Enrolled: 4

We are proud to announce that our student body continues to grow, with a current enrollment of 91 students. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to ensure each student receives the personalized attention necessary for academic success.

In October, we celebrated the graduation of 74 students who have successfully completed their programs at Empyrean Academy. We are confident that these graduates will make significant contributions to society in their future endeavors.

We regret to inform you that 35 students decided to withdraw from our academy during October. We wish them all the best in their future educational pursuits and hope they find a learning environment that suits their needs.

Currently, we have four cohorts enrolled at Empyrean Academy, each embarking on unique and challenging academic journeys. Our faculty looks forward to watching these students grow and develop throughout the school year.
