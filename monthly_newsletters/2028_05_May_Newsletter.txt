 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Monthly Newsletter - May 2028

Dear Parents and Guardians,

We are pleased to present the May edition of our monthly newsletter for the Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits. As we approach the end of another successful academic year, we would like to share updates on the growth and development of our school community.

Currently, our academy is actively serving 50 students across three cohorts. We are proud to report that as of this month, a total of 70 students have successfully graduated from our programs, demonstrating their commitment to intellectual pursuits and preparation for future endeavors. Simultaneously, we regret to inform you that 30 students have decided to leave our institution during the past month.

As always, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and personal growth. Our faculty remains focused on ensuring each student receives an exceptional education tailored to their unique needs and aspirations. We extend our gratitude to all those who have supported our students in their journey so far.

We look forward to continuing our work at Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits, fostering intellectual curiosity and empowering the next generation of leaders. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us directly.

Sincerely,
Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Administration
