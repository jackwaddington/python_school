 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Monthly Newsletter
Date: August 9, 2027

Dear Parents and Students,

We are pleased to share with you the latest updates from our esteemed institution, Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits.

This month marks a significant milestone as we graduate 46 students, while welcoming two new cohorts. Our active student count currently stands at 27, and unfortunately, we bid farewell to 27 students who decided to pursue other opportunities.

We extend our warmest congratulations to the graduating class of 2027! Your dedication and hard work have culminated in this impressive achievement. We wish you all the best in your future endeavors.

Our new cohorts, brimming with eagerness and potential, have already started their academic journey with us. We are excited to see them grow and flourish under our guidance.

We would like to remind all parents about the upcoming Parent-Teacher Meetings scheduled for next week. This is an excellent opportunity to discuss your child's progress and any concerns you may have.

Finally, we would like to express our gratitude to our devoted staff, students, and parents for their continued support. Together, we are shaping the future leaders of tomorrow.

Sincerely,
Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Administration
