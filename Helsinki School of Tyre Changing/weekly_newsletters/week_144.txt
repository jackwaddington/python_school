 THIS WEEK
It's been a week of contrasting emotions here at Helsinki School of Tyre Changing. We welcomed two new unit award recipients, Aisha Al-Khateeb and Sami Korhonen, adding their names to our ever-growing list of successful students. Yet, we also bid farewell to seven dedicated graduates who have shown remarkable progress and resilience in their journey towards mastering the art of tyre changing. With only 14 active students remaining, let's rally together for the upcoming week to ensure everyone reaches their full potential.

To our valued staff and students, congratulations are due to Aisha, Sami, and our latest graduates for their hard-earned achievements this week. However, we also share in the disappointment of those who had to leave prematurely. Let's remember their contributions as a reminder of what they've taught us. To our active students, let's push forward together, focusing on improvement and excellence in all we do.

NEXT WEEK
