Q1: What is the definition of tires?
  A) Flexible rubber covers that encase a wheel's rim to enable vehicle movement by adhering to a road surface
  B) Circular, rigid metal covers that encase a wheel's rim for vehicle movement
  C) Soft cushions placed on the seat for comfort during travel
  D) Electric motors used for propulsion
Answer: A

Q2: What does the recommended tire pressure for a typical passenger car usually equal at room temperature (in psi or bar)?
  A) 32 psi or 2.2 bar
  B) 45 psi or 3 bar
  C) 20 psi or 1.4 bar
  D) 60 psi or 4.1 bar
Answer: A
