 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
This month at the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, we have successfully graduated all 29 students who enrolled and filled our school's capacity to the brim! The enthusiasm and determination of these new tyre technicians were evident in their diligent practice and teamwork. We are thrilled with this month's results, further cementing our reputation as a leading institution for tyre changing education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to all 29 of our March graduates! In particular, we would like to recognize Anna Laaksonen and Petri Nurmi for consistently demonstrating exceptional skill and dedication throughout their courses in Tyre Change Basics and Tyre Mounting and Dismounting Skills. We wish them the best in their future careers as tyre technicians.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into April, we look forward to welcoming our next cohort of students for the upcoming courses. With two open spots due to this month's graduations, we encourage any interested individuals to apply and join our dynamic learning community. To ensure a smooth transition, we will focus on maintaining high-quality education while addressing any potential challenges that may arise as a result of increased demand for our courses.
