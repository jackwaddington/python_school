 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
This past month has been an exhilarating journey at the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing. With 75 graduates, our tire-changing community continues to expand and excel in their craft. Despite the 19 students who chose to pursue other paths, the school's capacity remains robust, ensuring we maintain our commitment to quality education in the ever-evolving automotive field.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to seven outstanding students: Ismail El-Husseini, Felix Hansen, Yara Al-Jaber, Emma Karlsson, Ali Patel, Gustavo Ramirez, and Sofia Olsen. Each of them demonstrated exceptional tire-changing skills and a strong work ethic that will serve them well in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into August, we anticipate the arrival of four new cohorts, bringing our student body to its full capacity. To ensure continued success for all students, we are focusing on improving accessibility to resources and increasing collaboration between students and instructors. We encourage all members of our community to remain dedicated to mastering their craft in the tire-changing field.
