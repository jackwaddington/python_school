 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
July has been a productive month for our school, with 52 graduates and a total of 29 active students. The enthusiasm and determination displayed by our learners have fostered a positive atmosphere conducive to learning. As we continue to expand our reach in the automotive industry, we're proud to see more students gaining practical skills that will enable them to excel in their careers.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to Ahmed Benyahia, Sven Olsson, Zara Patel, Yara Al-Khateeb, Lukas Zhang, Tiago Souza, Lukas Schmidt, Mehmet Yilmaz, Mohammad Hassan (both), Leon Li, and Lena Park for their successful completion of the program. Their mastery of advanced tyre swapping techniques and other skills will undoubtedly contribute to their success in this field.

LOOKING AHEAD:
With three new cohorts joining us in August, we're eager to maintain our high standards of excellence. The increased demand for tire-changing specialists calls for an even stronger commitment to providing comprehensive training and support for our students. We encourage everyone to embrace the challenges ahead and work together to build a brighter future in the automotive industry.
