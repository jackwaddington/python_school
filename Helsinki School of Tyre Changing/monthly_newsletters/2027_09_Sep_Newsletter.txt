 MONTHLY SUMMARY
September 2027 marked a significant milestone for Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with our largest graduating class to date of 29 students. The school's vibrant atmosphere and collaborative spirit have enabled us to reach this impressive achievement. Our student body continues to excel in their respective courses, demonstrating a passion for the field that is truly inspiring.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into October, our focus will be on ensuring that our remaining students receive the support and resources needed to thrive. With only two seats remaining for this academic year, we encourage those interested in tyre changing to consider enrolling for the upcoming term. Let's continue to foster an environment of learning and growth as we pursue excellence together in the ever-evolving world of automotive repair.
