 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
April was a vibrant month for the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with 40 graduates joining the ranks of our esteemed alumni. The energetic atmosphere in our workshops and classrooms was palpable as students mastered their tyre-changing skills, preparing to join the dynamic field of automotive services. Our graduating class this month includes Isabella Pereira, Aku Sullo, Ramiz Kumar, Juan Garcia, and Gizem Yilmaz - each demonstrating a remarkable dedication to learning and growth throughout their time here.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our five outstanding graduates! Isabella Pereira excelled in her tyre mounting and dismounting skills, showcasing a meticulous attention to detail that will serve her well in the industry. Aku Sullo consistently demonstrated a strong work ethic and keen problem-solving abilities, while Ramiz Kumar's adaptability and tenacity made him a valuable member of our community. Juan Garcia's natural mechanical aptitude shone through in his advanced tyre swap techniques, and Gizem Yilmaz's dedication to her studies and passion for learning ensured her success as she joins the workforce.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into May, we are excited to welcome a new cohort of students eager to embark on their tyre-changing journey. With 7 dropouts this month, we recognize the importance of providing a supportive and engaging learning environment for our students. Our dedicated staff is committed to continuous improvement and innovation in our curriculum, ensuring that each student receives the best possible education in the dynamic field of automotive services. We look forward to another fruitful month ahead!
