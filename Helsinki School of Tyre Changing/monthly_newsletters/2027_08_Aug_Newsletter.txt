August 2027 was a successful month for Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with all students from our two cohorts completing their courses. We are proud to have graduated 29 students, demonstrating the quality and effectiveness of our tire change education. With our school capacity at 20 seats, we are well positioned to continue delivering top-notch training in the dynamic field of automotive services.
