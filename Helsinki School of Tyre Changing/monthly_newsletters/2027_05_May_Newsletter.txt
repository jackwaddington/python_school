 MONTHLY SUMMARY
May 2027 has been a productive and satisfying month at the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing. With all 29 students successfully completing their courses, our student body has maintained its full capacity. The school's tire-changing fields provide an ideal environment for hands-on learning, fostering a positive atmosphere of determination and camaraderie among our students.

This month, we are proud to recognize three outstanding graduates: Sophie Lee, Isabella Pereira, and Lena Park. Their dedication, skill, and perseverance in the face of challenges set a high standard for future classes at our school. Congratulations, ladies!

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into June, we will focus on maintaining our strong enrollment numbers while continuing to provide exceptional instruction.
