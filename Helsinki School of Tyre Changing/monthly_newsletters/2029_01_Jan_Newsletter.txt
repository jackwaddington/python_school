 MONTHLY SUMMARY
January was a productive month for the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with 61 graduates and a total of 4 cohorts. Although we did see 18 dropouts, our active student count remains strong at 41. The school's tire field provided an engaging learning environment that allowed students to gain practical experience.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Mia Ren, from the Advanced Tyre Swap Techniques course, received a unit award this month for her exceptional dedication and mastery of the subject matter. We are proud of Mia's achievements and excited to see where her career in tyre changing takes her.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into February, we aim to maintain our high standards of education while addressing the challenges that come with accommodating more students. The school will work diligently to ensure each student receives a quality learning experience and achieves their personal best. We encourage everyone to continue striving for excellence in the tire field.
