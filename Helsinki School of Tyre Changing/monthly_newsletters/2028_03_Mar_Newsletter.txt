 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
This month has been a fruitful one for Helsinki School of Tyre Changing. With 45 active students and an impressive graduate count of 38, our school continues to thrive in the dynamic world of automotive maintenance. The passion and dedication shown by our students reflect positively on the quality of education we provide.

As we move into April, we face a challenging reality with only 20 seats available at our school. To accommodate the growing demand for our services, we need to expand our capacity. We encourage all tyre enthusiasts to consider joining us on this exciting journey of learning and growth. Let's continue to set new standards in the field of automotive maintenance!
