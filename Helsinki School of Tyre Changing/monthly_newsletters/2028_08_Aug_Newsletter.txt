 MONTHLY SUMMARY
August has been a productive month for Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with 58 graduates and an active student count of 23. The school's capacity was fully utilized, emphasizing the growing demand for skilled tyre changers in our field.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to Ada Sakari, Aiko Yamada, Sofia Gonzales, and Sami Korhonen who received unit awards at the Unit Award Ceremony on August 7th. Their dedication and hard work are a testament to the caliber of students we nurture at our school.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into September, we anticipate welcoming new cohorts to fill the 16 available seats in our courses. To maintain our high standards, we encourage all students to focus on their studies and practice diligently. We also extend a warm welcome to any prospective students who may be joining us. Let's continue to excel in the tyre changing industry!
