 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
December was a productive month at the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing, with an impressive number of students graduating and a steady influx of new enrollees. Our team excelled in various courses, particularly in Tyre Mounting and Dismounting Skills, demonstrating a strong interest in mastering these essential techniques. As we approach the cold winter season, we are reminded of the importance of proper tyre changing practices for ensuring vehicle safety on the icy roads of Finland.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, Ming Li and Ana Rodríguez successfully completed their coursework and graduated from our school. Ming's exceptional talent in Advanced Tyre Swap Techniques has earned him a well-deserved promotion at his workplace, while Ana's dedication to Tyre Change Basics has made her an asset to her team. LOOKING AHEAD:
As we enter the new year, our focus will be on maintaining our high standards and addressing the increasing demand for skilled tyre changers in the field. With three cohorts scheduled to start next month, we are looking forward to welcoming more students into the Helsinki School of Tyre Changing family. However, we must also address the challenges posed by the growing student population, ensuring that each individual receives the attention and resources they need to succeed. Here's to a productive and successful 2029!
