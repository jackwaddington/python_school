 MONTHLY SUMMARY
The month of February at Helsinki School of Tyre Changing was a productive one, with 47 active students and a total of 37 graduates. While we saw a slight increase in the number of dropouts, it is important to remember that every student's journey here is unique. Our field, the dynamic world of automotive maintenance, continues to offer us opportunities for growth and learning.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we proudly recognize Theresa Ng for her exceptional achievement in Advanced Tyre Swap Techniques. Her dedication and skill in the field are a shining example of what our students can accomplish here at Helsinki School of Tyre Changing.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into March, we aim to continue fostering an environment conducive to learning and growth. With three new cohorts joining us, we look forward to welcoming fresh faces to our school and helping them embark on their journey towards mastering the art of tyre changing. We also encourage all students to take advantage of our diverse course offerings and strive for excellence in their studies. Let's keep the momentum going!
