Q1: Which of the following best describes critical thinking?
  A) The ability to break down complex problems into smaller, manageable parts and reach a logical conclusion.
  B) A group-based method that encourages creative thinking and free association.
  C) The active process of analyzing and evaluating information, considering multiple perspectives, and making reasoned judgments based on evidence.
  D) Methods used to approach and solve problems effectively and efficiently.
Answer: C

Q2: Which strategy involves ranking alternatives based on their importance and relationships?
  A) Analytical hierarchy process (AHP)
  B) Decision tree analysis
  C) Brainstorming
  D) Lateral thinking
Answer: A
