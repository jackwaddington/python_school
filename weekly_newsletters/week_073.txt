 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter - May 24, 2027

Celebrating Week 73 at our esteemed institution, Empyrean Academy! We currently have 42 active students embarking on their intellectual journeys.

To date, we are proud to announce that we have graduated an impressive 32 students who have successfully navigated the path of knowledge.

As always, we remain committed to fostering a nurturing environment for all our learners. However, we also acknowledge the challenges some may face and regret to report that 26 students have chosen to leave our academy thus far.

We wish our departing students the best in their future endeavors and look forward to witnessing the continued growth and success of those who remain with us.

Stay connected, Empyrean family! Let's continue to inspire and be inspired.
