 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter - Week 163
Date: February 12, 2029

Dear Empyrean Community,

We are delighted to welcome another week at Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits! As of today, we have 69 active students embarking on their educational journey with us.

Since our last newsletter, we are proud to announce that 85 students have graduated and ventured into the world, equipped with knowledge and skills to make a difference. We wish them continued success in their future endeavors!

Unfortunately, we also had 46 students who decided to discontinue their studies with us. Our thoughts are with them as they pursue other paths, and we hope that their time spent at Empyrean has left an indelible mark on their lives.

In our ongoing commitment to excellence, we invite you to attend the upcoming Parent-Teacher Meeting on February 19th, where we will discuss student progress and set new goals for the remainder of the academic year. We look forward to seeing you there!

Warm regards,
Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Administration
