 Dear Parents and Students,

Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter - Week 55, January 18th, 2027

We warmly welcome you all back after the winter break! Our student body currently consists of 62 active students. To date, we have graduated a total of 28 students, and regretfully, 10 students have decided to leave our academy.

This week, we're excited to announce new elective courses starting next Monday: Introduction to Quantum Physics and Advanced Studies in Literature. We encourage all interested students to sign up at the front desk.

On Tuesday, our Debate Club will host an interschool debate competition. Come support our talented debaters as they take on other schools!

Wednesday marks a special Science Fair showcasing projects from various grade levels. This is an excellent opportunity for students to learn and share their findings with each other and the school community.

On Friday, we'll have an early release day at 1:00 PM. We invite families to join us for our annual Winter Showcase, where students can display their achievements and talents in music, dance, and art. The event will take place in the auditorium following dismissal.

Don't forget that the book club meeting is scheduled for next Thursday at 3:00 PM. This month, we'll be discussing "The Martian" by Andy Weir.

We hope you enjoy this week at Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits and look forward to seeing you all at our upcoming events!

Best regards,
Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Administration
