 Dear Empyrean Academy Community,

Week 93 at the Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits commences on October 11, 2027. We are excited to share our latest updates with you.

As of today, we have 64 active students currently enrolled in our programs. Since our inception, we have proudly graduated 58 talented individuals who have moved on to pursue their dreams. Regrettably, 28 students have chosen to leave our academy during the same period.

In this week, various workshops and seminars are scheduled for our students. Some of these include a coding bootcamp led by our guest instructor, Dr. Amelia Hart, a literature discussion group moderated by Professor Ravi Kapoor, and an art history class featuring works from the Renaissance period.

Our annual Charity Fair is also approaching! On October 28th, students will have the opportunity to participate in various activities, such as bake sales, silent auctions, and raffles, all proceeds of which will be donated to our chosen charity for this year: The Children's Literacy Foundation.

We encourage our community members to attend these events and support our students in their educational journey. For more information about our upcoming events or any questions regarding the Empyrean Academy, please visit our website or contact our office.

Wishing you all a successful and fulfilling week ahead!

Empyrean Academy Administration
