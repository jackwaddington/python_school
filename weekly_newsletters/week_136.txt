Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter - August 7th, 2028

We are now in our 136th week, and we are excited about the journey that lies ahead for all of our students. As of today, we have 96 active students at our academy, with a total of 71 graduates to date. Unfortunately, there have been 33 dropouts since our inception.

We would like to take this opportunity to remind you about our upcoming events:

1. Open House - On August 14th, we will be hosting an open house for prospective students and their families. This is a great chance for you to see what Empyrean Academy has to offer and meet some of our staff and students.
2. Science Fair - Our annual science fair will take place on September 8th. Students are encouraged to showcase their projects and demonstrate their scientific knowledge.
3. Back to School Night - Join us on October 6th for our Back to School Night. This event provides an opportunity for parents and guardians to learn about the curriculum, meet teachers, and ask questions.
4. Winter Concert - Our winter concert will be held on December 15th. Students from all grade levels will perform music and dance numbers for the community.

We are looking forward to another successful year at Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits. Thank you for your continued support, and we hope to see you at our upcoming events!

Best regards,
Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Administration
