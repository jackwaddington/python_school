 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter - September 3, 2029

Dear Parents and Guardians,

Welcome back! We are excited to kick off the new school year at Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits. As we embark on Week 192, we have 75 active students enrolled in our program. Since our founding, we have celebrated the graduation of 120 students and regrettably had 55 dropouts.

This week, our students will engage in a variety of stimulating activities designed to foster intellectual growth and curiosity. We are thrilled to introduce our new course offerings for this year, including "Artificial Intelligence Ethics," "Quantum Computing Basics," and "Space Exploration: The New Frontier."

We would like to extend our gratitude to the community for their continued support of Empyrean Academy. Your involvement helps make our school an exceptional learning environment where students can thrive and grow.

As always, if you have any questions or concerns regarding your child's progress, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to another successful year at Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits.

Sincerely,
The Empyrean Academy Team
