 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter - Week 97, November 8th, 2027

Greetings Empyrean Family,

We are thrilled to announce another successful week at our esteemed academy. As we progress through the autumn season, our active student count remains a robust 60. We extend a warm welcome to new students and invite returning ones to reconnect with their peers and faculty.

This past week saw six more of our dedicated scholars crossing the finish line, bringing our total graduates to an impressive 62. Congratulations to all our recent graduates on your incredible accomplishments! We are eager to see the positive impacts you will make in the world beyond Empyrean Academy.

In this week's update, we acknowledge that some of our students have faced personal or academic challenges and had to part ways with us. To date, there have been 28 students who chose to discontinue their educational journey at Empyrean Academy. We wish them the very best in their future endeavors and hope that they carry the skills and knowledge they've gained here forward.

We are grateful for the support of our valued community members, parents, teachers, and staff who contribute significantly to creating a nurturing environment that fosters academic success and personal growth at Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits.

Upcoming events:
- November 15th: Open House – Prospective students and families are invited to explore our campus and programs.
- November 29th: Thanksgiving Break – No school. Enjoy the holiday with family and friends!

Stay informed about school updates, events, and important dates by visiting our website at www.empyreanacademy.com or following us on social media. We look forward to another enriching week ahead!

The Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Team
