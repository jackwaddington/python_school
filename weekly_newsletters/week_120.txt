 Dear Empyrean Academy Community,

Week 120 at the Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits commences on April 17, 2028!

Currently, we are proud to have 51 active students engaged in our dynamic and enriching academic environment. To date, a total of 69 graduates have embarked on their post-academic journeys, making significant contributions in various fields. We also acknowledge the 30 students who have decided to pursue other paths, wishing them all the best in their future endeavors.

This week, we will be hosting a series of engaging workshops focused on the integration of cutting-edge technology and traditional arts. Our renowned guest speakers include Dr. Ada Lovelace and Leonardo da Vinci (our time-travel AI replica). Join us for a unique and memorable experience!

We are also excited to announce the launch of our new student-led initiatives, including the Environmental Sustainability Club and the Debate Society. These groups aim to foster critical thinking and social responsibility among our students while providing opportunities for collaboration and growth.

Please don't forget to mark your calendars for our annual Spring Festival taking place on May 5th! This event brings together the entire Empyrean family for an evening filled with delicious food, captivating performances, and friendly competitions.

We look forward to seeing you all at these exciting events and throughout Week 120. Remember, knowledge is a journey, not a destination - let's continue learning and growing together!
