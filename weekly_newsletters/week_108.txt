 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter
Week 108 - January 24, 2028

Dear Empyrean Community,

We are pleased to share updates from our vibrant learning environment as we approach the midpoint of the academic year. As of today, we have an active student body of 52 individuals, continuing on our mission of fostering intellectual growth and creativity.

To date, we have graduated a remarkable 69 students who have embarked on exciting journeys in various fields, contributing to society with their unique skills and knowledge gained at Empyrean Academy. We are incredibly proud of each one of them!

As we strive for academic excellence, it is worth noting that our community has experienced a modest change with 29 students choosing to pursue other opportunities outside Empyrean. While we miss their presence, we wish them all the best in their future endeavors and remain supportive of their decisions.

Our students' success stories continue to inspire us as they showcase their talents and achievements both inside and outside the classroom. We look forward to witnessing even more outstanding accomplishments in the coming months.

Please mark your calendars for upcoming events:
- January 26 - Science Fair Exhibition (Grades 9-12)
- February 03 - Annual Art & Literature Showcase (Grades K-8)
- February 09 - Parent-Teacher Conferences (All Grades)
- March 02 - Spelling Bee Competition (Grades 4-6)

We appreciate your continued support and participation in our school events. Together, we are building a community that nurtures curiosity, encourages innovation, and celebrates the joy of learning!

Warm regards,
The Empyrean Academy Team
