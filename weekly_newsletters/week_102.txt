 Dear Empyrean Academy Community,

Greetings from Week 102 at the Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits! As we approach the winter season, we are thrilled to share our latest updates with you.

As of December 13, 2027, we have 58 active students pursuing their dreams within our walls. To date, 64 graduates have successfully embarked on their post-academic journeys, making a significant impact in various fields. We are proud of each and every one of them.

Unfortunately, we also acknowledge the challenges that come with learning and growth. Currently, we have recorded 28 dropouts since our inception. Each student's departure serves as a reminder for us to continually improve and support our community.

In the coming weeks, we will be hosting several events to engage students and families, including an open forum discussion, a guest lecture series, and a charity drive to benefit local educational initiatives. Details will follow shortly in separate communications.

We appreciate your continued support and look forward to another enriching week at Empyrean Academy!

Warm Regards,
The Empyrean Academy Team
