 "Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter - December 14, 2026

Greetings Empyrean Family!

As we approach the end of another productive week at our academy, we are excited to share some updates with you.

These events provide our students with opportunities to showcase their skills and creativity while fostering a sense of camaraderie and teamwork.

As we prepare for the holiday season, we would like to express our gratitude to all members of the Empyrean community for your continued support and dedication. We look forward to another successful year filled with learning, growth, and achievement in 2027!
