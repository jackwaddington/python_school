 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter, January 25th, 2027

Dear Parents and Guardians,

We are delighted to welcome you to the 56th week of this academic year at Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits. Our active student count currently stands at 61, with 28 successful graduates and 11 dropouts to date.

This week, we would like to highlight the following events:

- The Model United Nations team participated in an interschool conference, bringing home several awards for their diplomatic skills and knowledge of global issues. Congratulations to all participants!
- Our chess club hosted a friendly tournament between students and teachers, with the event raising funds for school supplies and equipment. A big thank you to everyone who took part!
- The art department is hosting an exhibition showcasing student works from various classes, including painting, sculpture, and digital media. Don't miss this opportunity to appreciate the talents of our students!
- Finally, we remind all parents about the upcoming Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) meeting on February 1st at 6 PM in the auditorium. We encourage your attendance to discuss important school matters and share feedback.

We look forward to another enriching week of learning and growth at Empyrean Academy.

Best regards,
Empyrean Academy Administration
