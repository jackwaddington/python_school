 Dear Parents and Students,

Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter for the week ending November 29, 2027

Congratulations to our recent graduates! We are proud to announce that a total of 63 students have successfully completed their programs at Empyrean Academy so far.

As we move forward, our active student count remains 59. This week, we welcome new faces and bid farewell to those who have decided to pursue other opportunities outside the academy. To date, a total of 28 students have left the academy.

We would like to remind parents about the upcoming Parent-Teacher Conferences scheduled for December 6th and 7th. This is an excellent opportunity to discuss your child's progress and receive valuable insights from our dedicated faculty.

Looking ahead, we are excited to announce that our annual Science Fair will take place on January 10, 2028. Students are encouraged to start brainstorming their projects and reaching out to teachers for guidance.

Lastly, remember that the Empyrean Academy library is open to all students for research purposes. Books can be borrowed Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 4 PM.

We hope you have a wonderful week and look forward to seeing everyone at the Parent-Teacher Conferences!

Sincerely,
The Empyrean Academy Team
