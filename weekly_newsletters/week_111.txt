 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter - February 14, 2028

Greetings Empyrean Family,

We are now in our 111th week, and the momentum continues to build! As of today, we have an active student body of 52 students. We are proud to announce that since our inception, we have graduated a total of 69 students. On behalf of the entire Empyrean family, we congratulate each graduate on their outstanding achievements and wish them continued success in their future endeavors.

Regrettably, we also must acknowledge that we've had 29 students who chose to pursue other paths. While we are saddened to see them go, we appreciate the contributions they made during their time with us and wish them all the best in their future academic journeys.

This week, we would like to shine a spotlight on our exceptional faculty members:
- Mrs. Green: Teaching excellence award winner for her innovative teaching techniques in Mathematics and Sciences.
- Mr. Brown: Recognized for his dedication and leadership as the Dean of Students.
- Ms. Johnson: Honored for her tireless efforts in organizing various school events, ensuring a vibrant campus life.

We would also like to remind you about our upcoming event - The Annual Empyrean Science Fair, scheduled for March 7th. This event will showcase the outstanding work of our students and provide an opportunity for our community to engage with them. We encourage all parents, guardians, and friends to join us for this inspiring event!

Lastly, we would like to remind you that if you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to your child's teacher or the school administration. Our primary goal is to ensure a nurturing and supportive learning environment for all our students.

Thank you for choosing Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits as your partner in education. We look forward to another week of growth, discovery, and success!

Warm regards,
Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Team
