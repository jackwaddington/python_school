 Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Weekly Newsletter - Week 151
Date: November 20, 2028

Dear Empyrean Community,

Warm greetings from the Empyrean family! We are excited to share with you a recap of our latest week at your esteemed institution.

We have 84 active students currently enrolled and engaged in their intellectual pursuits. This week saw 2 new students joining us, adding to our vibrant learning environment.

We are proud to announce that this past week marked the graduation of 3 more students, bringing our total number of graduates to 76. Their hard work and dedication have not gone unnoticed and we wish them all the best in their future endeavors.

Regrettably, we also bid farewell to one student who decided to discontinue their education journey with us, taking our total count of dropouts to 40. We understand that each student's circumstances are unique and we wish them well on their academic path.

In addition to academics, students participated in various extracurricular activities, ranging from sports events to cultural programs. The school spirit was palpable as everyone contributed to making these events a success.

We would like to remind all parents about the upcoming Parent-Teacher Conference scheduled for December 4th. This is an excellent opportunity to discuss your child's progress and address any concerns you may have.

Finally, we encourage all students to continue striving for excellence in their studies and extracurricular activities. Let us cherish these formative years as a stepping stone towards greatness.

Thank you for your continued support and trust in Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits. We look forward to another enriching week ahead!

Best regards,
Empyrean Academy of Intellectual Pursuits Administration
