 MONTHLY SUMMARY
We have completed another productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 605 graduates and a total of 28 cohorts, it's clear our students are thriving in their pursuit of excellence in early childhood education and care. However, we regret the loss of 273 students who decided to leave our school this month. As we approach the winter season, let's maintain our dedication to nurturing the future educators and carers of our community.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into December, it's essential to maintain our focus on providing quality education and care for our remaining students. With only 21 seats available, we encourage those who are struggling to reach out to teachers for support, and for those who may be considering leaving, we invite you to reconsider the value of your education here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. Let's continue our journey towards shaping the future of early childhood education together.
