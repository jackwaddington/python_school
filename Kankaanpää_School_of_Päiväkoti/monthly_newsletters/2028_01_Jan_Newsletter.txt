 MONTHLY SUMMARY
We have concluded a challenging yet fruitful January, with 13 graduates joining the ranks of our esteemed Päiväkoti alumni. The school community is thriving, as evidenced by our robust enrollment figures across all courses. However, we must acknowledge the unfortunate departure of 42 students this month, a reminder of the complex journey each student embarks on in their pursuit of excellence in early childhood education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we congratulate nine remarkable individuals who have graduated from our program: Luis García, Oliver Nguyen, Amina Khan, Timur Aslanov, Aisha Kumar, Emma Lee, Benjamin Mendes, Léo Ngô, and Emma Wu. Each graduate has demonstrated a deep commitment to the field of Päiväkoti and we wish them the very best in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we turn the page to February, we are reminded that each new month brings new opportunities for growth. We invite all students to embrace these opportunities with renewed dedication and focus, as we continue to strive for excellence in early childhood education. Let us not forget our mission to create a nurturing, inclusive environment where every child can flourish. Together, we will face the challenges ahead with determination and resilience.
