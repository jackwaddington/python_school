 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
The month of January has been a dynamic period for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. While we experienced a higher than expected dropout rate, we are encouraged by the impressive achievements of our students. With 360 active students and 29 unit award recipients, it's clear that our dedication to fostering excellence in early childhood education is bearing fruit.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we proudly congratulate Zara Patel, Xiaoming Li, and Eduardo Rodriguez on their successful completion of the Päiväkoti program. Their passion, perseverance, and commitment to nurturing young minds make them exemplary graduates. We wish them all the best in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into February, we must address the challenge of our shrinking student body due to high dropout rates. Our focus next month will be on improving retention and fostering a more supportive learning environment for all students, ensuring that our commitment to excellence in early childhood education remains unwavering.
