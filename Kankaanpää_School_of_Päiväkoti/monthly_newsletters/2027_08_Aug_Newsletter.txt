 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
August has been a productive and challenging month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With a total of 324 active students, we've seen growth in several courses, particularly Early Childhood Education, Infant and Toddler Care, and Päiväkoti Teacher Training. However, we experienced a significant increase in dropouts, totaling 26 students. This is concerning and we are currently working on strategies to support our students and improve retention rates.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
No graduates this month. We look forward to celebrating our students' accomplishments next month.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into September, we will focus on addressing the challenges faced in August, including student retention and support. Our goal is to maintain a positive learning environment that fosters growth and success for all of our students. We encourage parents and guardians to stay engaged and involved with their child's education during this time.
