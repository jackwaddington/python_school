 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
August has been a month of change at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 294 active students, we have seen growth in our Early Childhood Education and Finnish Language and Culture courses. However, we are saddened by the 56 dropouts this month, as we strive to provide a nurturing environment for all students. Our field-based learning continues to be a valuable part of our curriculum, allowing students to connect theory with practice.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we did not have any graduates. However, we are proud of the progress made by our students and look forward to their continued growth in the upcoming months.

LOOKING AHEAD:
September brings new opportunities for learning and personal development. We encourage all students to embrace the challenges ahead and remember that every step taken is a step towards success. Let's work together to create an even more supportive and engaging environment at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
