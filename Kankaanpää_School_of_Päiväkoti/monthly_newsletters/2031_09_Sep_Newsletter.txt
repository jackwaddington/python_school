 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
September was a vibrant and productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 426 graduates, we celebrate our students' accomplishments in the field of early childhood education. Despite the high number of dropouts, our active student count remains robust, emphasizing the enduring commitment to quality Päiväkoti education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we proudly congratulate 33 graduates, including Rajesh Patel, Liam Leong, Lukas O'Sullivan, Ali Mohammed, Ricardo Fernandes, Emilie Nguyen, and Amina Khan. Their dedication to learning and growth in the Päiväkoti community will undoubtedly have a lasting impact on children's lives.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into October, our focus lies in supporting students who are new to Päiväkoti education while continuing to foster an inclusive and nurturing environment for all. We invite everyone to embrace the challenges ahead and look forward to another successful month of learning and growth at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
