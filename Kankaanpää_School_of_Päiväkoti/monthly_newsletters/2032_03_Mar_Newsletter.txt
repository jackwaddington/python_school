 MONTHLY SUMMARY
The month of March was a significant one for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 504 graduates, our school has continued to excel in nurturing the next generation of early childhood educators. However, it is concerning that 244 students decided to leave our program this month, highlighting the need for continuous improvement in student satisfaction and support. Our field, early childhood education, remains a critical one, especially in fostering a strong foundation for future generations.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to the 43 graduates who successfully completed their programs this month! Notable achievements include Elias Oliveira, Yara El-Assaad, and Mohammed Hassan, whose dedication and hard work have left an indelible mark on our school.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into April, our focus must be on addressing the reasons behind student dropouts and improving our support systems to retain more students. Additionally, we will continue to celebrate the successes of our graduates while welcoming new cohorts to Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. Let's strive to make April another month of growth and progress for our school community.
