 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
October was a busy month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 456 active students and 69 graduates. While we experienced a significant number of dropouts totaling 75 students, our school's capacity remains robust at 32 seats. The diversity of our student body continues to enrich our learning environment, fostering an atmosphere of collaboration and growth in the field of Päiväkoti education.
