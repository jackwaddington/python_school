 MONTHLY SUMMARY
October was a significant month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 29 students graduating and 40 receiving unit awards. The school's capacity was reached, indicating a high demand for our program. However, we experienced a higher than average number of dropouts, which is concerning and warrants further examination.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we proudly celebrate the achievements of 18 outstanding students: Fatima Benzakour, Aisha Kamal, Alejandro Gomez, Aisha Khan, Diana Silva, Zara Ali, Ana Dos Santos, Emilie Nguyen, Oliver Mendes, Yara Nazari, Ramy Nguyen, Leila Benjamin, Yara Gomez, Amina Kamara, Lucas Mendes, Yara El-Husseini, Hiroshi Takahashi, and Lukas Olsen. Their dedication and hard work have earned them well-deserved success in their field.

LOOKING AHEAD
In November, we anticipate an increase in enrollment as students seek to join our program for the next semester. To accommodate this growth and maintain our high standards, we will focus on recruiting qualified instructors, improving the learning environment, and addressing the reasons behind the recent rise in dropouts. We look forward to another productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
