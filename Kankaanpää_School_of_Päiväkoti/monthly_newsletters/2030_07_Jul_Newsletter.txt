 MONTHLY SUMMARY
July has been a productive month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 254 graduates and an active student body of 536. Despite the high number of dropouts at 160, our capacity remains at 26 seats. The diverse group of students and their passion for early childhood education has created a vibrant learning environment that continues to enrich our field.
