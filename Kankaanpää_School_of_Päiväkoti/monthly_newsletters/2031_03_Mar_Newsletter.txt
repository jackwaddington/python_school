 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
March at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti has been a busy and productive month, with 343 students graduating from various courses. The school's vibrant atmosphere continued to foster a nurturing environment for learning, despite the high number of dropouts reaching 202 this month. The school capacity remains at 26 seats, leaving room for new enrollments in April.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our exceptional graduates! Emma Lee, a standout student from Play-Based Learning Strategies, and Yusuf Omar, who excelled in Infant and Toddler Care, received Unit Awards on March 17th. We wish them the best in their future endeavors in the field of Päiväkoti education.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we approach April, we anticipate an increase in enrollments and are excited to welcome new students into our community. To maintain the high quality of education we provide, we encourage all staff members to focus on building strong connections with students, ensuring that each one feels supported and empowered in their learning journey.
