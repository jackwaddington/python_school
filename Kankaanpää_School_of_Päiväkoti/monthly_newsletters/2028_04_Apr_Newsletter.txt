 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This April, we welcomed 23 new graduates into the field of Päiväkoti education, while managing a total of 50 dropouts across our various courses. The school's vibrant atmosphere has continued to foster growth and learning among our 427 active students, especially in our Play-Based Learning Strategies and Early Childhood Education programs.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to Zara Ahmed, Leila Kim, Yara Park, Rohan Patel, Ayaan Ahmed, Fatima Al-Saidi, Elias Mendoza, Leon Lee, Adam Gómez, and Kai Lee who excelled in their studies this month and graduated from our Päiväkoti Teacher Training and Early Childhood Education programs. We wish them success as they embark on their new journeys in shaping the future of young minds.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we approach May, we encourage all students to stay focused and motivated, particularly in the Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti and Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education courses that have seen little engagement this month. Let's continue to work together towards fostering a nurturing environment where every child can thrive.
