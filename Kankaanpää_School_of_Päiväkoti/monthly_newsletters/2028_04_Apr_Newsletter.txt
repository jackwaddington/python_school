 MONTHLY SUMMARY
April has been a challenging month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 102 dropouts, we have seen a decline in student numbers to 388 active students. However, the Unit Award Ceremony was a highlight, recognizing the hard work and dedication of our students. Our school remains dedicated to nurturing future professionals in early childhood education and Päiväkoti care.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we proudly celebrate the graduation of 7 outstanding students: Emilie Nguyen, Elias Park, Mohammad Hussain, Lena Perez, Amina Khan, Amelia Dias, and Sofia Alvarez. Their passion for our field will surely make a positive impact in their future careers.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we enter May, our focus remains on strengthening our programs to better serve the needs of our students. With only 26 seats available in the school, we encourage those interested to apply promptly. Let's continue to foster an environment that encourages growth and success for all.
