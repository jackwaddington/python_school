 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
December has been a vibrant and productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with a total of 467 active students across our six courses. The energy and dedication displayed by our students have contributed to an engaging learning environment that fosters the growth of future educators in the field of early childhood education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
We are proud to recognize the achievements of 203 graduates, including Tomoya Takahashi, Mia Perez, Sophie Li, Emma Nguyen, Júlia Silva, Soyeon Kim, Leo Li, Ana Perez, Yusuf Ahmed, Mohamed Ahmed, Amina Khan, Yara Al-Said, Noah Wu, and many others who have completed their respective courses this month. We congratulate them on their success and wish them well in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into the new year, our focus will be on maintaining the high level of engagement that has defined December while addressing the challenges posed by the dropout rate of 130 students this month. By working together as a community to support one another and adapt to the ever-changing educational landscape, we are confident that Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti will continue to thrive in its mission to prepare dedicated and compassionate educators for the future.
