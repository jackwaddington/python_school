 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
December has been a month of growth and change at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 84 graduates and 57 students receiving unit awards, we celebrate the success of our students as they continue to excel in their fields. Simultaneously, we acknowledge the loss of 82 students who have chosen to discontinue their education with us. As we approach the end of the year, we are reminded of the importance of our mission to nurture future educators and caregivers in Päiväkoti.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we proudly acknowledge 25 graduates who have demonstrated exceptional dedication to their studies. Among them are Zara Yousafzai, a compassionate and empathetic early childhood educator; Lukas Sakamoto, an innovative designer of Päiväkoti curricula; and Lena Park, who has consistently exhibited strong leadership qualities in her play-based learning strategies course.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into the new year, our focus will be on providing a supportive and engaging environment for our current students while continuing to attract and nurture future Päiväkoti educators. We understand the challenges that lie ahead, particularly with the recent dropout rates, but we are committed to addressing these issues head-on. Together, we will work towards creating a more sustainable and successful educational experience for all.
