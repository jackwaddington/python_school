 MONTHLY SUMMARY
December was a mixed month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with an active student body of 349, but unfortunately, we saw high dropouts totaling 211. This has left us with vacancies in some courses, which we are actively filling for the new year. Despite these challenges, our students have continued to excel in their studies, particularly in Early Childhood Education, Finnish Language and Culture, and Child Psychology in Päiväkoti.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Twelve students graduated this month, including Juan Rodriguez, Sofia Li, Mia Li, Yara Gómez, Tomás González, Aaron Zhang, Sofia Lopez, Mia Lee, Sophia Zhang, Mohammed Al-Hassan, Max Kumar, and Li Na Zhang. We are proud of their achievements and wish them the best in their future endeavors within our field.

LOOKING AHEAD
With the new year approaching, we will be refocusing on attracting more students to fill vacant seats in our courses. Additionally, we plan to invest more resources into supporting student success and fostering a positive learning environment for all. We look forward to continued growth and development at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
