 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
This month was a transformative period for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with the active student count reaching 463, and 171 students graduating. However, we also faced an unexpected increase in dropouts, amounting to 116 students. This dynamic reflects the ongoing evolution of our school community and the challenges that come with it, particularly as we strive to provide high-quality Päiväkoti education.

Congratulations to our 28 graduates: Oliver Mendez, Oliver Ng, Sara Hassan, Yara Guzman, Yara Al-Saidi, Aisha Kamal, Sophia Zhang, Zara Patel, Ahmed Mohammed, Mariana Ramirez, Nora Akter, Liam Li, Ahmed El-Husseini, Noah Park, Noah Li, Lukas Olsen, Charlotte Wu, Sofia Diaz, Emilie Nguyen, Tamara Johnson, Leonardo Dos Santos, Lukas Pereira, Isabella Dos Santos, Sofia Mendes, Ethan Gomez, Aisha Khan, Amina Khan, and Mei-Li Wang. Each of them has demonstrated commitment and passion in their field, contributing significantly to the Päiväkoti community during their time here.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we enter October, our focus will be on addressing the recent challenges, particularly the rise in dropouts. We aim to provide additional resources for students who may require extra support and encourage open communication between teachers and students to ensure a positive learning environment. We also look forward to welcoming new students joining our community next month. Let's continue to foster an inclusive, supportive atmosphere where each student can thrive in their Päiväkoti journey.
