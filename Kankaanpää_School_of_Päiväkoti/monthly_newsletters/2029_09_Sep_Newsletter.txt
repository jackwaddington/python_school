 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
September has been a transformative month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 80 graduates and 249 dropouts, we've seen both growth and change within our school community. The dedication and hard work of our students are evident in their accomplishments. As we continue to foster the principles of Päiväkoti education, we look forward to another month of learning and development.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our 18 graduates who have demonstrated exceptional commitment and passion for their chosen fields. A special mention to Ryuichi Tanaka, Zara Wang, Ibrahim Mohammad, Oliver Johansson, Isabella Garcia, Oliver Diaz, Aisha Patel, Sofia Lee, Yara Patel, Aisha Kamal, Amina Khan, Amina Kamara, Yara Al-Said, Leonardo Gonzalez, Ivan Ivanov, Amina Khan, and Noah Díaz. LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into October, we encourage all students to maintain their focus on learning and personal growth. With a decreased student body, we hope to create more opportunities for individualized attention and collaboration within our school community. Let's continue to support each other as we strive towards success in the field of Päiväkoti education.
