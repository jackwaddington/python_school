 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
October has been a dynamic month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 372 active students, we've seen a steady flow of learning and growth in our community. However, we experienced a higher-than-usual number of dropouts, totalling 28 students, which we are currently addressing to ensure the best possible educational experience for all. As our students continue to excel in their respective courses, their passion for Päiväkoti education remains evident.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we did not have any graduates. We eagerly anticipate celebrating our students' achievements next month.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into November, we will focus on refining our curriculum to better accommodate the needs of all students. We are excited for the upcoming Päiväkoti Teacher Training course, which commences next month. We encourage every student to take advantage of this opportunity and strive for success in their respective courses. Let's continue to nurture young minds together!
