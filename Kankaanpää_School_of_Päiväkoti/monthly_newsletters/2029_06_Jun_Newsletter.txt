 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
This past month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti has been a whirlwind, with 62 students graduating and 241 choosing to pursue other paths. Although it's always bittersweet to see our students move on, the school remains resilient with 397 active students continuing their journeys in Early Childhood Education, Finnish Language and Culture, Päiväkoti Curriculum Development, Health and Safety in Päiväkoti, and Play-Based Learning Strategies. Our Päiväkoti community's spirit remains strong as we gear up for another fruitful month ahead.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we proudly recognize the achievements of 8 graduates who have left their mark on our school – Yara Al-Khateeb, Ethan Patel, Ibrahim Oluwatomiwa, Ella O'Sullivan, Zara Patel, Leonardo Ribeiro, Amina Khan, and Hector García. Their contributions to the Päiväkoti field will undoubtedly inspire future generations of educators.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we turn the page to July, we are eager to welcome new students and continue fostering a nurturing environment for our returning pupils. We anticipate another month filled with learning, growth, and the pursuit of excellence in Early Childhood Education. However, with the recent increase in student enrollment, we must remain vigilant about ensuring that each student receives the individual attention they deserve. Let's rise to the challenge and make the most of this new academic chapter together!
