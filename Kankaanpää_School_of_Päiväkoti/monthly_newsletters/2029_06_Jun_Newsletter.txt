 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
This June marks a significant milestone for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti as we bid farewell to 143 graduates and welcome 245 new students. With our active student body now numbering 451, our commitment to shaping the future of early childhood education remains undeterred. We are proud to be a hub for nurturing talent in the field, fostering a community that values inclusivity, creativity, and continuous learning.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our 35 graduates from the Class of 2029! Among them are accomplished individuals like Amina Kamara, who demonstrated exceptional dedication in Päiväkoti Teacher Training, and Liam Lee, a shining star in Early Childhood Education. Each graduate leaves us with lasting memories and a strong foundation for their future endeavors in the world of education.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we step into July, our focus remains on providing an enriching learning experience for all students. With limited seats available (32), we encourage new students to familiarize themselves with our curriculum and engage actively to make the most of their time at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. We also look forward to introducing new courses, including Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti and Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education, in the coming months to further enhance our students' education. Together, we continue to shape the future of early childhood education, one student at a time.
