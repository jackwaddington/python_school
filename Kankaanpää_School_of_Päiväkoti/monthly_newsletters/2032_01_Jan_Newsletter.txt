 MONTHLY SUMMARY
January was a dynamic month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With an active student count of 533, we saw an engaging exchange of ideas and a strong commitment to our field. Despite a high dropout rate of 239 students, the remaining pupils demonstrated resilience and determination, ensuring a positive school spirit.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to the 478 dedicated graduates who left their mark on Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti this month. Among them were Hiroshi Nakamura, Tomás García, Zara Patel, Yara Silva, Ibrahim Diop, Oliver Mendez, Sara Mohammed, and many others who excelled in their respective courses.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into February, our focus will be on enhancing student retention and fostering a supportive learning environment for all. We encourage students to maintain their passion for early childhood education, Päiväkoti teacher training, and related fields, as they continue on their educational journeys.
