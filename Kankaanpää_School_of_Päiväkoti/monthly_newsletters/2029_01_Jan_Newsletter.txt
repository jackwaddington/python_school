 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This January has been an eventful month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With a total of 474 active students, we have witnessed a mix of growth and change as 91 students graduated and 85 chose to pursue other paths. The school's vibrant atmosphere continues to foster learning and development in the field of early childhood education and care.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to our outstanding graduates: Emilia Kim, Ethan Gomez, Aisha Kamal, Yuna Nguyen, Sophie Kim, Oliver O'Sullivan, Ahmed Hassan, Lena Perez, Aisha Khan, Chloe Lee, Raquel Silva, Lukas Olsen, Emma Lee, Tomás García, Sofia Lopez, and many more. Your dedication to the field of Päiväkoti has not gone unnoticed, and we wish you all the best in your future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we step into February, our focus will be on maintaining a high standard of education while supporting our current students as they progress through their courses. We also look forward to addressing the areas where we saw challenges this month and continuing to foster an inclusive and supportive learning environment for all. Let's make the most of the remaining winter months together!
