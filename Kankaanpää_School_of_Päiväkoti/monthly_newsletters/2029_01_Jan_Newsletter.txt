 MONTHLY SUMMARY
January at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti saw a dynamic blend of learning and growth, with 45 graduates joining our esteemed alumni. Despite the high number of dropouts, we maintain an active student body of 389, affirming our commitment to fostering quality education in early childhood care.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month's Unit Award Ceremony honored outstanding students like Isabella Garcia, Hector García, Mohammad Ahmed, Yara Al-Khateeb, Sofia Lee, Ricardo González, and many more for their dedication to the Päiväkoti field.

LOOKING AHEAD
February demands a renewed focus on retaining students while ensuring quality teaching across all our courses. We look forward to another month of growth and success at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
