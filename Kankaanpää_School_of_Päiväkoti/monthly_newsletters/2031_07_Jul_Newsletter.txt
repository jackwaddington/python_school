 MONTHLY SUMMARY
We witnessed a productive and engaging month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 393 graduates, our students' dedication to early childhood education and care is evident. However, the high number of dropouts (214) raises concerns about student retention and warrants further investigation.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into August, let us focus on improving student retention while continuing to celebrate the successes of our students. Let's work together to create an even more supportive learning environment that fosters growth and encourages perseverance in Päiväkoti education.
