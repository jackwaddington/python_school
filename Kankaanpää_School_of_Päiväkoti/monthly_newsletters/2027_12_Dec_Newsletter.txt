 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
As we bid farewell to December, we reflect on a month marked by both growth and change. With 352 active students, we are eagerly engaging in our mission to foster early childhood education and Päiväkoti teacher training. However, the loss of 37 students is a significant setback. We remind ourselves that the field of Päiväkoti is challenging and rewarding, and we remain committed to supporting each student's unique journey.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we celebrate the achievements of eleven dedicated individuals who have successfully completed their programs: Mira Lee, Max O'Sullivan, Hannah Doe, Amina Kamara, Juan Ramírez, Ivan Petrov, Zuzana Novakova, Isabella Diaz, Hiroshi Tanaka, Sofia Li, and Marcus O'Sullivan. We wish them the very best as they embark on their new roles in the field of early childhood education.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into January, we are looking forward to renewed energy and dedication from our students and staff. The coming month will present challenges, but with a strong community and a shared commitment to excellence in Päiväkoti education, we are confident that together, we can navigate the road ahead.
