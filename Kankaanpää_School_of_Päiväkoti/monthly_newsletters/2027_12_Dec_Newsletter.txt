 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This December marked a significant change in our school community, with an influx of new students and the graduation of one exceptional individual. While we celebrated the successes and accomplishments of many, we were also saddened by the departure of 78 students. As we move forward, it is crucial to maintain our commitment to providing a nurturing environment for early childhood education in Päiväkoti, while ensuring that each student's unique needs are met with the utmost care and dedication.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to Yara El-Said, who successfully completed her studies this month. Her passion for learning and dedication to creating a safe and engaging environment for children will undoubtedly be an asset in her future endeavors. We wish her the best of luck as she embarks on a new chapter in her journey.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we prepare for the New Year, it is essential that we continue to focus on our core values and strive for excellence in all aspects of our educational program. We encourage our students to maintain their passion for learning and to remember that every day presents new opportunities for growth. With 8 cohorts starting next month, we look forward to welcoming the new faces and fostering a positive and supportive environment where they can thrive. Let us embrace the challenges ahead with determination and optimism.
