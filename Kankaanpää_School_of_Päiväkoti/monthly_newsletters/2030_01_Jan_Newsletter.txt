 MONTHLY SUMMARY
January 2030 has been a productive and transformative month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. We welcomed 507 active students, saw 210 graduates embark on their journeys in early childhood education, and unfortunately, 133 students decided to leave our community. The success of our students continues to inspire us, reflecting the dedication and hard work of our esteemed faculty. Our focus remains on providing a nurturing environment that fosters growth, creativity, and compassion — cornerstones of Päiväkoti education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we celebrated the achievements of 55 outstanding students who received Unit Awards, including Mateo Diaz, Amina Khan, Santiago Gonzalez, Sophie Park, Hiroshi Nakamura, Anais Nguyen, and many more. Their dedication to learning and commitment to our shared values make us proud and optimistic about the future of early childhood education.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into February, we will focus on addressing the decrease in student numbers and finding new ways to attract and retain students. We encourage all members of our community — teachers, staff, and parents alike — to continue supporting one another as we work together to create a brighter future for Päiväkoti education.
