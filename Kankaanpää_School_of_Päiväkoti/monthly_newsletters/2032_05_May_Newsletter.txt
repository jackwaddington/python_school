 MONTHLY SUMMARY
The month of May has been a remarkable journey for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With an impressive graduating cohort of 525 students and a decrease in the number of dropouts, we are proud to see our students thriving in their respective fields. The school's capacity is fully utilized with active students numbering 525, demonstrating our commitment to providing quality education in the field of Päiväkoti.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we celebrate graduates such as Roberto Sánchez, Lukas Nielsen, Lukas Söderström, Isaac Dos Santos, Taro Sakurai, Emilia Jimenez, Xiang Li, Marcus Nguyen, Amina Khan, Zainab Hussein, and many others who have excelled in their courses. Their hard work and dedication to the field of Päiväkoti is commendable.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into June, our focus will be on maintaining this positive momentum. With a new cohort joining us next month, we are excited to continue shaping future leaders in the field of early childhood education and Päiväkoti. However, we also recognize that challenges may arise as we strive for continuous improvement. We urge all members of our school community to embrace these challenges with resilience and determination.
