 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This December, our school community experienced a busy and productive month. With 461 graduates and an active student body of 502, we are proud to have reached capacity in our 26 seats. The energy and enthusiasm of our students continue to enrich our learning environment as they dive deep into the field of Päiväkoti education.

LOOKING AHEAD
Next month, we will continue to focus on supporting our students' success, particularly in courses with low enrollment such as Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti and Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education. We encourage everyone to take advantage of these important offerings and help foster a more inclusive and effective learning environment for all students at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
