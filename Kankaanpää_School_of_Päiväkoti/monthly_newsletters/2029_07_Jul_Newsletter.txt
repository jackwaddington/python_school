 MONTHLY SUMMARY
July was a productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 490 active students and 151 graduates. While we experienced a high number of dropouts at 109, the school's capacity remains at 32 seats. The diverse range of courses offered continues to attract a broad student demographic, fostering an engaging learning environment within our field of Päiväkoti education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to our graduates who demonstrated excellence in their respective fields this month: Yuna Nakamura (she/her) in Child Development in Päiväkoti, Lena Park (she/her) and Zara Ali (she/her) in Infant and Toddler Care, and Lukas O'Sullivan (he/him) in Early Childhood Education. We wish them all the best as they embark on their new journeys.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into August, we must continue to adapt and innovate to meet the needs of our students. With a focus on addressing the challenges posed by recent dropouts and strengthening our curriculum, we aim for another successful month ahead in the world of Päiväkoti education.
