 MONTHLY SUMMARY
The July of 2029 marked an eventful period at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With a total of 436 active students, the school community thrived as they embarked on their respective journeys in Early Childhood Education, Finnish Language and Culture, Päiväkoti Curriculum Development, Child Psychology in Päiväkoti, Health and Safety in Päiväkoti, Play-Based Learning Strategies, and Päiväkoti Administration. While we bid farewell to 244 students who chose to leave the program, it was a significant achievement for 70 of our students who graduated this month.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we enter August, our focus lies on welcoming new students and supporting returning ones as they continue their educational journey at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 26 available seats remaining, we anticipate a busy month filled with exciting learning opportunities and continued growth for all involved. We encourage everyone to embrace the challenges ahead and to make the most of every day in this enriching field of Early Childhood Education.
