 MONTHLY SUMMARY
The past month has been a time of growth and change for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 459 active students, we've seen a strong commitment to early childhood education and Päiväkoti teacher training. However, we were saddened by the departure of 60 students and the lack of participation in courses such as Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti and Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education. As we continue to foster an inclusive environment, we will strive to address these gaps in our curriculum.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
We celebrated the achievements of 8 graduates this month: Sven Schmidt, Isabella Rodriguez, Liam Li, Max Nguyen, Mohammed Nguyen, Amina Khan, Sofia Lopez, and Ana Gomez. Each of them demonstrated a deep understanding of early childhood development and Päiväkoti curriculum design, setting an impressive standard for future graduates.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into August, we will focus on providing additional support to our students in challenging areas, encouraging active participation in all courses, and continuing to foster a nurturing, inclusive environment for all. We look forward to another month of growth and learning together.
