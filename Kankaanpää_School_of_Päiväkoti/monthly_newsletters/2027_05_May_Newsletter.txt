 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This month marked a slight dip in student numbers, with 278 active students remaining after the departure of 22. While this is disappointing, it allows us to focus more closely on the needs of our existing cohorts. The school's vibrant atmosphere continues to thrive, nurturing future educators and providing exceptional care for our young learners within the field of Päiväkoti education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Regrettably, we had no graduates this month. However, we remain optimistic that our dedicated students will continue their diligent work towards achieving success in their chosen courses.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into June, we encourage all students to embrace the opportunities for growth and learning that await them. The challenges are many – from coursework demands to managing personal responsibilities – but with dedication and perseverance, we can ensure a bright future for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti and its valued community.
