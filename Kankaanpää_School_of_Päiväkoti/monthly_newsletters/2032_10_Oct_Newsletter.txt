 MONTHLY SUMMARY
October was a productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 590 graduates and an active student body of 542. The school's capacity has been pushed to its limits, demonstrating the high demand for education in our field. We look forward to the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Several outstanding students graduated this month. Among them are Emma Lee, a dedicated student of Child Development in Päiväkoti, who consistently demonstrated a deep understanding of child development principles; Mia Kim, another standout from the Play-Based Learning Strategies course, known for her innovative approach to teaching; and Zainab Hussein, who excelled in our Päiväkoti Teacher Training program.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into November, it is crucial that we maintain our high standards of education while addressing the increasing demand for seats. We will focus on expanding our capacity to ensure we can continue to provide quality education to all aspiring educators.
