 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
December has been a mixed month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 172 active students, we have continued to foster a nurturing environment for young learners in our community. However, it is with regret that we saw 28 students choose to leave our school this month, leaving us with 32 empty seats as we approach the new year. Despite these challenges, we remain dedicated to providing high-quality Päiväkoti education and look forward to a fresh start in January.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, no students graduated from our school. We encourage our current students to continue striving for excellence as they work towards their own graduation.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we enter January 2027, we face a challenging task of filling the empty seats left by our departing students. To meet this challenge, we will be focusing on recruiting new students and offering incentives to help attract families who value quality Päiväkoti education for their children. We remain committed to providing a supportive learning environment where every child can grow and thrive. Let's welcome the new year with renewed energy and determination!
