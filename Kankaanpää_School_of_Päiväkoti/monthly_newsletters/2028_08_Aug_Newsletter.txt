 MONTHLY SUMMARY
The past month has been a rollercoaster at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 20 graduates stepping into their next chapter and 191 students choosing to move on. Despite the substantial departures, our dedicated team continues to nurture and educate 339 students across various disciplines, including Early Childhood Education, Finnish Language and Culture, Päiväkoti Curriculum Development, Child Psychology in Päiväkoti, Health and Safety in Päiväkoti, and Play-Based Learning Strategies.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we bid farewell to 20 exceptional students who have made their mark on our community. Among them are Taro Yamada (Child Psychology in Päiväkoti), Max Kumar (Child Psychology in Päiväkoti), Leonardo Ribeiro (Päiväkoti Curriculum Development), Benjamin García (Finnish Language and Culture), Yara Al-Khateeb (Early Childhood Education), Oliver Wu (Early Childhood Education), Zara Patel (Early Childhood Education), Emilia Olmos (Early Childhood Education), Juan Rodriguez (Health and Safety in Päiväkoti), and Yara Gómez (Health and Safety in Päiväkoti). We wish them the very best in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into September, our focus remains on quality education and providing a welcoming environment for all. With only 26 seats available, we encourage prospective students to apply early and look forward to welcoming new cohorts to Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. In the meantime, we'll continue striving to foster a nurturing, supportive community that promotes academic excellence in our field.
