 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
August has been a vibrant and productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 445 active students, we are witnessing the growth of our Päiväkoti community. While it's regrettable that 65 students decided to leave our school, we remain committed to providing a nurturing and enriching environment for all children. Our capacity expansion efforts continue to reflect the growing interest in quality early childhood education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we celebrated 40 remarkable graduates who have successfully completed their programs. Notable among them are Miraam Abdalla (she/her) from Infant and Toddler Care, Amina Kamara (she/her) from Play-Based Learning Strategies, and Emma Lee (she/her) from Päiväkoti Teacher Training. Their passion, dedication, and commitment to children's education serve as an inspiration for our entire community.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we transition into September, we will be focusing on increasing enrollment in courses like Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti and Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education to better support our students and teachers. We look forward to another month of growth, learning, and fostering a strong sense of community within the Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti family.
