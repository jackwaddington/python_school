 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
The month of July has been a mix of successes and challenges. With 136 active students, we've seen a vibrant learning environment in our Päiväkoti classes. However, it is concerning to note the loss of 14 students due to various reasons. Despite this setback, the Unit Award Ceremony on July 13th was a heartwarming celebration, honoring 15 outstanding students. Their accomplishments serve as a reminder of our commitment to nurturing young minds in the field of early childhood education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Regrettably, we had no graduates this month. We eagerly look forward to our next round of successful students who will take their first steps into their careers as qualified educators.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into August, it is crucial that we continue to support and inspire our current students while working diligently to address the reasons behind the recent dropouts. Together, we can ensure a strong start to the new academic year, ready to welcome new faces and build upon our achievements.
