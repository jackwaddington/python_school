 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
Despite a challenging month with 33 student dropouts, the Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti community remains steadfast in our mission to foster early childhood education and cultural understanding. With 217 active students, we are reminded that every departure represents a unique individual leaving our nurturing environment, but also an opportunity for new beginnings.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we did not have any graduates. However, we eagerly anticipate the continued growth and success of our dedicated students as they work towards completion of their programs.

LOOKING AHEAD:
Next month, we will focus on addressing the recent dropouts, engaging with our community to understand their concerns, and working diligently to ensure a welcoming and supportive environment for all our students. We will also strive to expand course offerings in areas such as Health and Safety in Päiväkoti, Play-Based Learning Strategies, Art and Creative Activities for Children, and Päiväkoti Administration, to better serve the diverse needs of our students and the field at large.
