 MONTHLY SUMMARY

We welcomed January with a bustling school full of energy and eagerness to learn. With 236 active students, our classrooms buzzed with the joy of discovery and growth. However, we experienced a setback as we bid farewell to 14 students who chose to pursue other paths. Despite this loss, we remain committed to upholding our mission in nurturing future Päiväkoti educators.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we celebrated the achievements of 20 dedicated students who received Unit Awards in various courses. Mira Lee, Yara Park, Aisha Kamal, Amina Kamara, Sarah Patel, Yusuf Ng'ang'a, Leon Lee, Ricardo Diaz, Oliver Sánchez, Sofia Lee, Amina Mohamed, Noah Wu, Liam Li, Mohammed Nguyen, Lukasz Wojciechowski, Emma Cho, Yara Alvarez, Mohammad Khan, Emma Nguyen, and Liam Lee all demonstrated their dedication and hard work in the field of Päiväkoti education.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we embark on February, we encourage our students to remain focused and committed to their studies. The upcoming months will be crucial as we prepare for course assessments and final projects. We also invite parents to stay involved and supportive during this challenging yet exciting period in our students' educational journey. Let us continue to grow together in the nurturing environment of Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
