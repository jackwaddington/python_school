 MONTHLY SUMMARY
The month of February has been a productive one at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 332 graduates and 520 active students. The vibrant atmosphere is testament to the dedication of our students and staff. The high number of unit awards indicates an increasing focus on practical application in our field of early childhood education.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into March, we are looking forward to continuing our focus on practical application and student success. We recognize the need for improvement in courses such as Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti and Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education, and will be working diligently to address these challenges. We anticipate a busy month as we strive to provide the best possible education for our students and continue to build upon our reputation as a leader in early childhood education.
