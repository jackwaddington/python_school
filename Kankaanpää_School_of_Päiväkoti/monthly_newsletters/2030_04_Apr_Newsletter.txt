 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
April has been a productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 522 active students and 231 graduates. While we saw a slight increase in dropouts compared to previous months, our dedicated faculty and staff remain committed to providing high-quality education in the field of early childhood development.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our 231 graduates, including Yara Al-Saidi (Infant and Toddler Care), Amina Kamara (Play-Based Learning Strategies), Lukas Söderlund (Play-Based Learning Strategies), Emma Larsen (Child Development in Päiväkoti), and many more. Their hard work, dedication, and passion for the field will undoubtedly make a positive impact on the lives of children and families.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into May, we encourage our students to continue their hard work and commitment to learning. We also acknowledge the need for improved strategies in addressing challenging behaviors among our students and look forward to addressing this issue by offering workshops and discussions focused on managing these behaviors effectively. Let's keep striving for excellence together!
