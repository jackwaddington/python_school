 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
March has been a month of significant growth and achievement for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 507 active students, we've seen a vibrant educational environment that fosters the development of future educators in our field. The high number of graduates, 659, is a testament to the success of our programs and the dedication of our students. However, the loss of 284 students through dropouts is a concern we must address, as it challenges our capacity to maintain optimal learning environments for all.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we celebrated the accomplishments of numerous students during the Unit Award Ceremony. Among them were Nina Morales, Sofia Lee, Omar El-Khatib, Liang Zhang, Leonardo Silva, Zoe Lee, Sebastián Gutiérrez, Satoshi Nakamoto, Maliha Ahmed, and many more. Their hard work and dedication exemplify the spirit of our school and set a high standard for future graduates.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into April, we must maintain our focus on providing quality education while addressing the challenges posed by dropouts. We will be introducing new initiatives to support students who may be struggling and ensuring that all programs continue to meet the needs of our growing community. We look forward to another month of learning and growth at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
