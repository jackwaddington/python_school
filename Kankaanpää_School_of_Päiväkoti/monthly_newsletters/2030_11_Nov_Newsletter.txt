 MONTHLY SUMMARY
November has been a productive and engaging month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 298 graduates, we celebrate the success of our students who have honed their skills in various aspects of Early Childhood Education and Päiväkoti education. Although we have also experienced 180 dropouts this month, it is important to acknowledge each student's journey and encourage continued growth. Our field remains committed to providing a nurturing environment that fosters success and empowers our students to make meaningful contributions in their future careers.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to the 298 graduates who excelled in their respective courses this month! In particular, we commend Rajesh Patel, Aisha Khan, Rizwan Ahmed, and Siyabonga Ntshangase for their exceptional achievements in Play-Based Learning Strategies. Their dedication to nurturing the young minds of tomorrow is truly inspiring.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we transition into December, our focus lies on maintaining a supportive learning environment while addressing the dropout rate. Our school's capacity remains at 26 seats, and we encourage all students to remain committed to their studies and make the most of this opportunity in Päiväkoti education. We also look forward to celebrating the accomplishments of our remaining students as they approach graduation.
