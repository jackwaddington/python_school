 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
April has been a challenging month for our school community, as we bid farewell to 235 students and welcomed only 0 new ones in Päiväkoti Administration. With 57 graduates joining the ranks of our alumni, we are reminded of the transformative nature of our field and the impact we strive to make on young minds. Our capacity has dwindled to 26 seats, which is a concern as we look forward to serving the needs of future generations.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Forty-nine exceptional students have been recognized for their hard work and dedication this month. Among them are Noa Choi, Nina Nguyen, Sofia Olsen, Rafael Sanchez, Zara Wang, Xinran Wang, Oliver Johansson, Sara Al-Khateeb, Hugo Dos Santos, Isabella Das, Aisha Khan, and many more who have shown exceptional growth throughout their journey with us.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into May, our focus must be on attracting new students to fill the gaps left by those who have graduated or moved on. We are also dedicated to providing support for our current students as they continue their journey in Päiväkoti and work towards their future successes.
