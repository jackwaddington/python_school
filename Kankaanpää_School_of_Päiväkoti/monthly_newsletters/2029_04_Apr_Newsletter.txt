 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
April was a significant month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 477 active students, 124 graduates, and 99 dropouts. This highlights the dynamic nature of our school community and underscores our commitment to fostering growth in early childhood education. The increased number of unit award recipients reflects our students' dedication and hard work in their respective fields.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to 124 graduates who completed their courses this month, including Tamara Johnson, Yara Guzman, Sophia Zhang, Mehdi Benali, Ana Gomes, Sophie Li, Soyeon Kim, Yusuf Omar, Ivan Kim, Anais Nguyen, Alejandro López, and many others. Their success underscores the quality of education we provide in our unique Päiväkoti setting.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into May, we look forward to continuing our mission of providing excellent early childhood education. We encourage students to remain focused and engaged, and we invite new students to join us as we strive for excellence in all that we do. Let's continue to make a positive impact on the lives of young children together!
