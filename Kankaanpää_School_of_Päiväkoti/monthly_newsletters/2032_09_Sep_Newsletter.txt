 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
September has been a productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with a total of 555 active students and 579 graduates. Despite the high number of dropouts at 266, we are proud to see our school capacity utilized effectively. Our diverse student body continues to excel in various courses, including Early Childhood Education, Päiväkoti Teacher Training, Infant and Toddler Care, Play-Based Learning Strategies, Child Development in Päiväkoti, and Päiväkoti Curriculum Design.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we celebrate the success of several students who received unit awards. Notably, Hosni El-Sayed (he/him) and Yara Silva (she/her) for their exceptional work in Child Development in Päiväkoti, Omar Ben-Hur (he/him) and Sanaa El-Bashir (she/her) for their dedication to Infant and Toddler Care, and Maria Gonzalez (she/her) for her achievements in Play-Based Learning Strategies.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into October, our focus will be on maintaining a positive learning environment and supporting those students who may be struggling. We encourage all students to continue their hard work and dedication to the field of Päiväkoti education. Let's strive for an even more successful month ahead!
