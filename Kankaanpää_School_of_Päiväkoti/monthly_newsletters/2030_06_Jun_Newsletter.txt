 MONTHLY SUMMARY
The month of June has been a vibrant mix of learning, growth, and transition at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With a record 496 active students, the school's lively atmosphere echoed with the spirit of education in early childhood development. However, the high number of dropouts (156) indicates a need for more comprehensive support and resources to ensure each student's success. Our field-based learning approach continues to be a valuable asset in nurturing well-rounded Päiväkoti professionals.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to the 248 graduates who completed their courses this month! A special acknowledgment goes to Julia Pereira, Yuna Nguyen, Lukas O'Sullivan, Jacob Lee, and Lukas Schmidt for their exceptional performance in Play-Based Learning Strategies; and Amina Kamara, Aiden Lee, Lukas Olsson, Sofia Lee, Anna Wang, and Mateo Diaz for their outstanding work in Early Childhood Education. We are proud of your achievements and look forward to seeing you excel in the field of Päiväkoti!

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into July, it's essential to focus on addressing the high dropout rate and provide additional resources to support our students. Additionally, we will be launching new courses in Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education and Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti. We encourage all students to continue embracing the spirit of learning and to push their boundaries for a successful month ahead!
