 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This past month has seen a steady pace at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 449 active students diligently engaging in their studies. While we experienced a slight increase in dropouts, we are proud to have graduated 73 students, many of whom excelled in various courses like Child Development in Päiväkoti and Early Childhood Education. The school's field continues to foster an environment that nurtures growth and learning for our students.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Among the graduates this month were İdris Al-Husseini, Ritvik Sharma, Oliver Ng, Ana Rodrigues, Oliver Wilson, Sophia Zhang, Noah Li, Ana Gomes, Leyla Akyol, Sara Yang, Santiago Ramirez, Mia Park, Lena Perez, Emma Cho, and many others who demonstrated outstanding commitment and dedication to their Päiväkoti education. We wish them the best in their future endeavors!

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into December, it is essential that we address the decline in enrollment for courses like Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti and Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education. We invite students to consider these important areas of study and look forward to another fruitful month of learning together.
