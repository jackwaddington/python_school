 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
November was a significant month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, as we saw the successful completion of 37 student journeys and welcomed 164 new students into our programs. Although we experienced a high number of dropouts this month (209), it is important to remember that each departure presents an opportunity for us to improve and grow. As we move forward in our mission to shape the future of early childhood education, we remain dedicated to providing a nurturing and enriching environment for all our students.
