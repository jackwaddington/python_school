 MONTHLY SUMMARY
As the December snowflakes have started to fall, our Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti community has continued to grow and flourish. With 504 active students, we've witnessed a strong focus on early childhood education, infant and toddler care, and Päiväkoti teacher training this month. Although we faced challenges with only one cohort enrolled in 'Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti,' our dedicated faculty and students have shown resilience and determination.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, 309 graduates proudly completed their courses with us, including Elena Perez (Child Development in Päiväkoti), Yara Al-Saidi (Play-Based Learning Strategies), Amina Khan (Child Development in Päiväkoti), and many others. Each graduate has contributed significantly to our school's mission, and we wish them continued success in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we look forward to the new year, we anticipate expanding our offerings and welcoming more students into our Päiväkoti community. Our focus for January will be on strengthening the cultural awareness and inclusion curriculum to better serve a diverse student body. We'll also work closely with our faculty to ensure they have the resources necessary to continue delivering high-quality education in their respective fields. Let's make 2031 another successful year at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti!
