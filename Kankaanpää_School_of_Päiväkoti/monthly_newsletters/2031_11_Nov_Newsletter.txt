 MONTHLY SUMMARY
We have closed another productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with a total of 515 active students, reflecting our continued growth and dedication to early childhood education. Although we faced a higher dropout rate this month, it is important to remember that each student's journey is unique. Our focus on play-based learning strategies and child development has yielded success stories, with 450 graduates this month.
