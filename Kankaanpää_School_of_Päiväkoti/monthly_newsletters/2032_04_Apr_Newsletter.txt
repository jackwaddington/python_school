 MONTHLY SUMMARY
April was a productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 518 graduates and a total of 248 dropouts. The active student count remains high at 534, demonstrating the school's continued commitment to early childhood education and teacher training. This month's focus on various courses such as Early Childhood Education, Infant and Toddler Care, Play-Based Learning Strategies, and Päiväkoti Curriculum Design has fostered a vibrant learning environment.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to the 68 students who received unit awards this month! Among them are Nina Wu, Liam Zhang, Mehmet Özdemir, Zara Patel, and many others who demonstrated exceptional dedication and growth in their respective courses. We wish them continued success in their future endeavors in the field of Päiväkoti education.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into May, our focus will be on maintaining the positive momentum from April while addressing areas where improvements can be made, such as enrollment in courses like Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti and Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education. We look forward to another productive month of nurturing future educators in the field of early childhood education.
