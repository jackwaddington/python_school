 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This August, we welcomed 431 active students, with a total of 73 graduates and 246 dropouts. We are pleased to see the continued interest in our Päiväkoti courses, particularly Early Childhood Education, Finnish Language and Culture, and Päiväkoti Curriculum Development. However, we are concerned about the high number of dropouts and will be exploring ways to better support students through their programs.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to our 36 graduates! Particularly notable is Lukas O'Sullivan, who excelled in both Päiväkoti Curriculum Development and Early Childhood Education, and Nabil El-Assaad, who demonstrated exceptional understanding in Health and Safety in Päiväkoti. We wish them all the best in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD
Next month, we will be focusing on improving student retention by offering additional support services, such as tutoring and mentorship programs. Additionally, we are planning to add a new course - Art and Creative Activities for Children - in response to the growing demand for well-rounded Päiväkoti education. We look forward to another productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
