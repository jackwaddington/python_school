 MONTHLY SUMMARY
August 2029 marked a significant transition at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 477 active students, we welcomed new learners while bidding farewell to 111. The school's vibrant atmosphere continued to inspire growth and success in our diverse cohorts.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Several of our dedicated students reached the culmination of their studies this month, including Maximilian Olivier, Juan González, Isabella Gomez, and Yara El-Assad, who demonstrated outstanding achievements in Play-Based Learning Strategies, Early Childhood Education, and Päiväkoti Teacher Training respectively.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we prepare for September, we anticipate a dynamic new semester filled with fresh ideas and the continued pursuit of excellence. We invite all students to focus on our school's core values of cultural awareness, inclusion, and nurturing play-based learning, as these pillars have been at the heart of our success this August.
