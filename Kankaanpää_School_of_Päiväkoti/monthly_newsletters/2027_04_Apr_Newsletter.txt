 MONTHLY SUMMARY
We have bid farewell to 42 students this month, marking a significant change in our school community. Despite the loss, we welcome 6 new cohorts and maintain an active student body of 216. The school spirit remains strong, with the students engaging enthusiastically in their courses, particularly Early Childhood Education and Finnish Language and Culture.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, no students graduated from Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. We eagerly anticipate celebrating our graduates' achievements next time around.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into May, our focus is on supporting the new students settling into their courses and providing a nurturing environment for them to thrive. We also aim to address the under-enrollment in certain courses such as Health and Safety in Päiväkoti, Play-Based Learning Strategies, Art and Creative Activities for Children, and Päiväkoti Administration, encouraging more students to participate. Let's continue to foster a strong sense of community and learning together.
