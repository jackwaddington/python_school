 MONTHLY SUMMARY
April has been a busy month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 282 active students engaged in our various courses. The school's vibrant energy is palpable, particularly in Early Childhood Education and Päiväkoti Teacher Training programs. However, we are saddened by the loss of 18 students who have decided to pursue other paths. Our capacity remains at 40 seats, ready to welcome new learners.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we celebrate 26 students who received Unit Awards in Early Childhood Education, Päiväkoti Teacher Training, Infant and Toddler Care, and Play-Based Learning Strategies. Their dedication and hard work are truly inspiring.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we transition into May, our focus lies on nurturing the growth of our remaining students while exploring ways to fill the seats left vacant by recent dropouts. We look forward to seeing our students continue their journey in Päiväkoti education and welcome new members to our community.
