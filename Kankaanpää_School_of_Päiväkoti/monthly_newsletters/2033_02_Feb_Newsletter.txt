 MONTHLY SUMMARY
February has been a dynamic and productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 647 graduates, we are celebrating the successful journey of our students who have embraced the principles of early childhood education and Päiväkoti teaching. Simultaneously, it's important to acknowledge the 284 dropouts, as we strive to provide support for every student to complete their course successfully. As our capacity remains at 21 seats, we look forward to welcoming new learners in the coming months.
