 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
March has been a productive month for the Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with a total enrollment of 484 students across our various courses. While we saw a high number of graduates (224), it's noteworthy that we also experienced a higher than usual dropout rate (142). As the school year progresses, we aim to address this trend and continue fostering a supportive learning environment for all our students in the field of Päiväkoti education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to 263 graduates who completed various courses this month! Notable achievements include Noah Zhao, Xiao Li, Mohammad Ahmed, Søren Jensen, Sophie Park, Sofia O' Sullivan, and Zuzana Nováková. Their hard work and dedication serve as an inspiration for our current students to strive for success in their own studies.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we enter April, we encourage our students to remain committed to their studies and make the most of this educational journey. In particular, we will be focusing on improving student retention rates and offering additional support services to address any challenges that may arise. Let's continue fostering a strong community in Päiväkoti education together!
