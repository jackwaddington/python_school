 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
February was a dynamic and productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 513 active students, 494 graduates, and unfortunately 243 dropouts. Despite the challenges faced, our dedicated team continued to deliver quality education in the field of Päiväkoti, fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment for all students.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Amongst this month's graduates were 62 outstanding students who demonstrated exceptional dedication and commitment to their chosen courses. We are particularly proud of Mehak Singh (she/her) from Infant and Toddler Care, Lena Perez (she/her) from Play-Based Learning Strategies, and Ibrahim Akbar (he/him) from the same course, who each shone brightly in their respective fields.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into March, we will focus on continuing to support our active students while striving for improvements in student retention. Our goal is to further enhance the learning experience at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti by addressing challenges and fostering a more engaging and inclusive environment for all.
