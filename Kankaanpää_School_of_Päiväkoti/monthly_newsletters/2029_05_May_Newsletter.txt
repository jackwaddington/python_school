 MONTHLY SUMMARY
The month of May has been a whirlwind at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 61 graduates and 402 active students, we continue to foster the growth of young minds in our nurturing environment. The school spirit has remained strong despite the high number of dropouts this month. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to providing an enriching learning experience within the capacity limits of our institution.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into June, our focus is on strengthening our curriculum offerings to accommodate the demand for Early Childhood Education and Päiväkoti Curriculum Development courses. We aim to fill the vacant seats in the Päiväkoti Administration course and rejuvenate the Art and Creative Activities for Children course. The upcoming challenges present us with opportunities for growth, and we remain steadfast in our commitment to nurturing tomorrow's leaders.
