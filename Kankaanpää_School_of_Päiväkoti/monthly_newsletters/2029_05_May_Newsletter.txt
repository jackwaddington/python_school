 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
This past month has been a remarkable one for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 51 students receiving unit awards and a total of 136 graduates. While we regret the loss of 101 students, it's important to note that this month's high graduation rate underscores our commitment to fostering growth in the field of Päiväkoti education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we celebrate the accomplishments of several outstanding students. Among them are Abigail Oluwadare, who excelled in Päiväkoti Teacher Training, and Lukas Müller, who demonstrated exceptional skill in Infant and Toddler Care. Their dedication and hard work will undoubtedly make a positive impact in their future endeavors within the field of Päiväkoti.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we look towards June, we anticipate another busy month filled with learning and growth for our remaining students. With only 32 seats available, we encourage all students to focus on their studies and make the most of this opportunity. We also urge everyone to remain committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment that fosters success in Päiväkoti education.
