 MONTHLY SUMMARY
February was a significant month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 101 graduates and a total of 457 active students. While we saw a higher number of dropouts this month, our school capacity remains at 32 seats. The enthusiasm in our classrooms, particularly during the Unit Award Ceremony on February 19th, underscores the dedication of our students to their chosen fields and the importance of early childhood education.

LOOKING AHEAD
March will require our continued focus on improving student retention, as well as ensuring that courses are meeting the needs of our diverse student body. The upcoming Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) meeting on March 15th promises to be an invaluable opportunity for us to hear feedback and work together towards a more supportive learning environment. Let's build upon this month's successes as we continue to foster a love for learning and growth at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
