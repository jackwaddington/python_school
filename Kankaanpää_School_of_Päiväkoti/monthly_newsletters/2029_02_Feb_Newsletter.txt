 MONTHLY SUMMARY
February was a significant month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 48 graduates and a total of 382 active students. However, we also experienced a high number of dropouts, amounting to 220 students this month. This trend raises concerns about student satisfaction and the overall health of our school community.
