 MONTHLY SUMMARY
Despite a significant number of dropouts, we are proud to have successfully graduated 545 students this month, reflecting the dedication and commitment to excellence in early childhood education. With a total of 550 active students and a school capacity of only 21 seats, we continue to strive for quality learning experiences that foster personal growth and professional development within our Päiväkoti community.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we enter August, let's embrace the new opportunities for growth and learning that await us. We encourage all students to take on challenges with confidence and perseverance, especially in the areas where we saw lower enrollment this month, such as Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti and Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education. Together, we will continue to cultivate a nurturing and inclusive environment that fosters the development of the whole child within our school community.
