 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
This past month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti has been a mix of success and challenge. With a total of 14 graduates, we are proud to see our students achieving their goals and moving forward in their careers. However, we also experienced a significant number of dropouts, totaling 108 students. As we look ahead, it's clear that our focus must be on providing support and resources to help every student thrive in our Päiväkoti program.

Their passion for early childhood education, Finnish language and culture, curriculum development, child psychology, health and safety, play-based learning strategies, and administration has left a lasting impact on our school community.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into July, it's crucial that we address the high number of dropouts experienced this month. We will be working closely with our students to identify areas where they may need additional support, and implementing new strategies to improve overall engagement and success in our Päiväkoti program. We are excited for the opportunities that the coming months will bring, and look forward to continuing our mission of nurturing future leaders in early childhood education.
