 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This month has seen a notable shift in our student body, with 26 graduates and 55 dropouts. The active student count remains high at 419, reflecting the continued interest in Päiväkoti education. Our school's nurturing environment, focused on child development and play-based learning strategies, continues to be a beacon for those passionate about early childhood education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to our 26 graduates this month! Among them are Lena Perez (Päiväkoti Teacher Training), Tiago Santos (Infant and Toddler Care), Amina Kamara (Child Development in Päiväkoti, twice!), and Sofia Mahdavi (Play-Based Learning Strategies). Each of these individuals has demonstrated a deep commitment to their chosen field and will undoubtedly make a lasting impact as they embark on their careers.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into July, we must focus on filling the 55 vacated seats and ensuring that new students are well-equipped to thrive in our unique learning environment. Our dedicated staff will work tirelessly to provide a supportive transition for these students, fostering their growth and success throughout the coming months. We look forward to another fruitful period of education and personal development at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
