 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This month has been marked by both progress and change. With 108 graduates, our community of learners is growing stronger, while we also bid farewell to 94 students who chose to pursue other paths. The school's capacity remains at 32 seats, providing an opportunity for new enrollees in the upcoming months. The strong presence of our students in various courses indicates a collective dedication to Päiväkoti education and its field.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into April, our focus will be on maintaining the quality of education while ensuring a seamless transition for new students entering our programs. We invite all prospective learners to explore the diverse offerings at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, and look forward to another month of growth and development in our community.
