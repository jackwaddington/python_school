 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
March has been a significant month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 54 students graduating from our programs. However, we also faced a challenging decrease in student numbers, as 229 students dropped out. This shift emphasizes the importance of continued support and engagement within our community to uphold the high-quality education we strive for in the field of Päiväkoti.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
We are proud to highlight our graduating class of 14 exceptional students who have completed their Päiväkoti journey this month: Leila Mohammadi, Yara El-Said, Taro Yamada, Leonardo Santos, Liam Zhang, Ehsan Nouri, Noah Lee, Lukas Olsen, Liam Leung, Léon Duval, Aya Saito, Oliver Nguyen, Yannick Mbenga, and Ibrahim Oluwatoyin. Their dedication and hard work have not gone unnoticed and we wish them continued success in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into April, we anticipate an increased focus on student retention and academic performance, aiming to provide a supportive environment that encourages the ongoing growth and development of our students. We will also continue to work diligently to fill our available seats with enthusiastic learners who share our passion for the Päiväkoti field. Let's embrace this new month with renewed energy and determination!
