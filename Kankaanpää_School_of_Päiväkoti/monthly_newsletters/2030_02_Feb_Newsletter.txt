 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This February has been a busy and productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With an enrollment of 497 students across our various courses, we've witnessed active engagement in the field of early childhood education. Despite a slight increase in dropouts, we are encouraged by the progress made by our students and the strong bond they share with their peers.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to the 19 graduates who successfully completed their programs this month: Sarah Wu, Aisha Patel, Isabella Rodriguez, Lena Perez, Kaori Matsumoto, Amina Kamara, Leila Hossain, Lukas Olsen, Aisha Kumar, Noah Tanaka, Zuzana Novotná, Sophie Lee, Hamza El-Amin, Zara Patel, Ricardo Gutierrez, Ana Perez, Tomás García, Júlia Silva, and Sophie Li. LOOKING AHEAD
As we enter March, we encourage our students to maintain their focus and determination. The upcoming events such as the Unit Award Ceremony and graduation ceremonies provide excellent opportunities to showcase their skills and growth. We also anticipate addressing the dropout issue proactively by providing additional support for those who may be struggling, ensuring a successful journey for all at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
