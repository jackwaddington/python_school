 MONTHLY SUMMARY
September proved to be a transformative month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 24 graduates and an active student body of 331, we have seen both growth and challenges. This dynamic mix has fostered a vibrant learning environment, reflecting the essence of our field — early childhood education and care.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
We are proud to acknowledge the achievements of Zara Patel (she/her), Ethan Patel (he/him), Maximilian Schmidt (he/him), Isabella Chen (she/her), Sofia Olsen (she/her), Sofia Li (she/her), Mia Park (she/her), and Aisha Khan (she/her). Their dedication, passion, and contributions to our school have made a lasting impact on both their peers and our community.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into October, our focus is clear: strengthening our school's capacity to support the remaining 165 students while ensuring they continue to thrive in their chosen courses. We will also be actively recruiting new students for Päiväkoti Administration and Art and Creative Activities for Children, expanding our offerings to better serve the needs of Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti and its community.
