 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This September, we welcomed 433 active students to Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. Although we experienced a challenging month with 68 dropouts, our dedicated team of educators continues to foster an engaging and nurturing learning environment. The field of early childhood education is one that requires constant adaptability, and this month has been no exception.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to the 49 graduates who completed their respective courses this September! Among them were Lukas Sakamoto (Child Development in Päiväkoti), Leila Mehdi (Infant and Toddler Care), Oliver Mendez (Play-Based Learning Strategies), Mohammad Khan (Child Development in Päiväkoti), Aisha Kamal (Play-Based Learning Strategies), Zuzana Novakova (Play-Based Learning Strategies), Lukas Schmidt (Play-Based Learning Strategies), and many more. We wish them all the best in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into October, our focus lies on improving student retention and ensuring that each student feels supported in their Päiväkoti journey. To address the challenges faced this month, we are evaluating current programs and implementing new strategies to better serve our diverse student body. We look forward to another month of growth and learning at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
