 MONTHLY SUMMARY
September has been a vibrant and engaging month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 280 graduates marking significant achievements in their respective fields. The active student count remains strong at 550, while we regrettably saw 170 dropouts. As we move forward, we will strive to continue providing an enriching and nurturing environment that supports our students' personal and professional growth.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations are in order for our outstanding graduates, including Taro Yamada, Maximilian Silva, Sara Al-Khalidi, Sophie Kim, Maximilian Müller, Rosa Díaz, Zara Al-Khateeb, Tomás González, Sanaa El-Bashir, and many more. Their hard work, dedication, and passion for Päiväkoti education are truly inspiring.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we enter October, our focus will be on ensuring that all students receive the support they need to excel in their studies. We will also continue our efforts to foster a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. We anticipate challenges ahead but are confident that, together, we can meet them with resilience and determination.
