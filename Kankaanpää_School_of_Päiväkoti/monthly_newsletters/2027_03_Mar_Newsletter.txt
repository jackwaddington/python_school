 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
The month of March has been a challenging one for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, as we witnessed the departure of 34 students. Despite this setback, our dedicated team of educators continued to nurture and inspire the remaining 182 active students. The school's field continues to serve as a rich learning environment, where children can grow and develop in a supportive and stimulating atmosphere.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we did not have any graduates. We eagerly await the culmination of our students' journeys with us, and are confident that those who remain will continue to thrive.

LOOKING AHEAD:
April brings new opportunities for growth and learning at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. As we welcome new students and say farewell to departing ones, we remain committed to providing an exceptional educational experience in the nurturing environment that our field offers. We look forward to overcoming the challenges posed by recent changes and continuing to support the success of all our students.
