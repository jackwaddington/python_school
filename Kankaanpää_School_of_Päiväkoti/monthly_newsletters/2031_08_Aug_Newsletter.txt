 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
August has been a significant month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 404 graduates and a vibrant learning environment that saw 525 active students. As we reflect on the numbers and the mood, it's clear that our school is thriving, especially in courses such as Early Childhood Education, Infant and Toddler Care, and Päiväkoti Teacher Training. Our students continue to impress us with their dedication and passion for Päiväkötiede.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we celebrate the accomplishments of several outstanding graduates, including Noah Lee (Child Development in Päiväkoti), Soyeon Kim (Infant and Toddler Care), Karen Wang (Infant and Toddler Care), Lena Perez (Infant and Toddler Care), and Emma Nguyen (Play-Based Learning Strategies). We wish them all the best in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into September, our focus will be on addressing the unexpected dropout rate, ensuring that our curriculum remains engaging and relevant to our students' needs, and fostering an even more inclusive learning environment for all. We are excited about the opportunities that lie ahead as we continue to shape the future of Päiväkötiede together.
