 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This October has been an eventful month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With a total of 537 active students, we are proud to see our community growing and thriving. Despite the high number of graduates (287) and dropouts (176), it is essential to remember that each student's journey is unique, and we continue to support those who remain with us in our mission to foster excellence in early childhood education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Twenty-seven students proudly graduated this month, including Xiao Li (they/them), Emma Larsen (she/her), Tomás García (he/him), and more. Their achievements serve as a testament to the hard work and dedication they've shown throughout their time with us. We wish them all the best in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into November, it is crucial that we maintain our focus on quality education while addressing the increasing demand for seats. With only 26 available seats in our school, we encourage prospective students to apply early and look forward to welcoming new faces to our Päiväkoti community next month.
