 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This October has been a vibrant and productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 437 graduates, we are delighted to see the positive impact our educational programs continue to have in shaping future educators and care providers. The high number of dropouts (230) is concerning and we will be addressing this issue to ensure our students receive the support they need to complete their courses successfully.
