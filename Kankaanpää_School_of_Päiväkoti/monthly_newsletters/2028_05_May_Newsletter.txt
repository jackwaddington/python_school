 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This month has been a challenging one for us at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with a notable increase in dropouts and a decrease in student enrollment. However, we are proud to have graduated 25 students, each demonstrating the skills and passion needed to excel in their chosen fields. These graduates embody the essence of our school's commitment to quality Päiväkoti education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to our 25 graduates who completed their courses this month! Among them are Yuna Nakamura, Rafael Gonzalez, Sofia Li, and Hiroshi Tanaka, who have shown exceptional dedication and talent in their respective fields. We wish them all the best in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into June, our focus will be on maintaining a balanced student body while ensuring that each student receives the individual attention they need to succeed. We encourage new students to join us and look forward to another month of growth and learning at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
