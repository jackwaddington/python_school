 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This May has been a period of growth and change for our Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti community. While we saw an unfortunate increase in student dropouts, we are proud to report that our active student count remains high at 383. The success of our students in various courses demonstrates the dedication and commitment they bring to their studies.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into June, we will focus on addressing the challenges that led to the high number of dropouts this month. We are committed to supporting our students and ensuring they have the resources and opportunities they need for continued success in their Päiväkoti journey. Let's work together to make next month even better!
