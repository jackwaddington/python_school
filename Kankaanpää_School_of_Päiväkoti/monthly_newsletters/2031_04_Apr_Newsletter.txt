 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
April has been a busy and rewarding month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 533 active students and 36 graduates, our school community continues to grow and flourish. The diversity of our student body is reflected in the wide range of courses offered, from Early Childhood Education to Päiväkoti Teacher Training. While we have seen some dropouts, it's important to remember that every student's journey is unique. Our focus remains on providing a nurturing and inclusive environment where all students can learn and grow.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our 36 graduates! Among them are Mia Park, who excelled in Play-Based Learning Strategies, and Aisha Kumar, who demonstrated exceptional skills in Early Childhood Education. We wish them all the best as they embark on their next adventures.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into May, our focus will be on maintaining our high standards while continuing to support each student's individual needs and aspirations. We are looking forward to welcoming new students and continuing our mission of fostering a love for learning in the field of Päiväkoti education.
