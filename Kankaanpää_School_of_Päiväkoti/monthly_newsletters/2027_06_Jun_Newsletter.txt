This past month has been a rollercoaster, with 277 active students and 0 graduates. We've seen the positive impact of our diverse student body in various courses such as Early Childhood Education, Päiväkoti Teacher Training, Infant and Toddler Care, Play-Based Learning Strategies, Child Development in Päiväkoti, and more. However, we experienced a setback with 23 dropouts, highlighting the need for ongoing support to ensure each student's success. We also look forward to seeing the continued growth and success of our students during the upcoming Unit Award Ceremony. Let's continue to strive for excellence together!
