 MONTHLY SUMMARY
December was a busy and productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. We saw a total of 506 active students, 617 graduates, and unfortunately 277 dropouts. Despite the challenges, our team continued to deliver high-quality education in Early Childhood Education, Infant and Toddler Care, Play-Based Learning Strategies, Child Development in Päiväkoti, Päiväkoti Teacher Training, and Päiväkoti Curriculum Design.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to our 81 graduates who received Unit Awards on December 20th! Among them were Zuzana Nováková (Play-Based Learning Strategies), Sara Park (Play-Based Learning Strategies), Tomás Gómez (Child Development in Päiväkoti), and Julius Tuominen (Infant and Toddler Care). Their dedication, hard work, and commitment to the field of Päiväkoti education is an inspiration to us all.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into the new year, we are committed to addressing the challenges that have arisen, particularly in managing challenging behaviors in our students. We will be focusing on improving our resources and training for teachers in this area, with the goal of providing a more supportive and nurturing environment for all students. We appreciate your continued support as we strive to excel in Early Childhood Education.
