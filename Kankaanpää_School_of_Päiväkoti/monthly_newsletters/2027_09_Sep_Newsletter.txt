 MONTHLY SUMMARY
We ended September with 232 active students, a slight decrease from our starting numbers due to the natural attrition rate. The school's warm and nurturing environment continues to foster growth and learning, as evidenced by the impressive performance of our students in various courses this month. Our focus on holistic development, both academically and emotionally, remains strong, ensuring that each child thrives within the Päiväkoti field.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
There were no graduates this month as we are an educational institution primarily focused on early childhood education and related courses. Our students are still growing and learning, and we eagerly anticipate their progress in the coming months.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into October, our focus will be on maintaining a positive and engaging atmosphere while continuing to address the needs of our diverse student body. We encourage parents and guardians to remain involved in their child's learning journey, and we look forward to another month filled with growth and success.
