 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
September at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti was a month of growth and challenges. With an active student count of 324, we welcomed new cohorts into our diverse learning community. However, we also experienced the unfortunate departure of 26 students, highlighting the need for continued support in navigating the rigors of Päiväkoti education. Our field-based approach to teaching and learning remains a beacon of innovation within early childhood education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we did not have any graduates. We eagerly anticipate our students' successes in the coming months.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we transition into October, we are committed to fostering an even more supportive and inclusive environment for all students. Particular focus will be placed on strengthening our approach to managing challenging behaviors and promoting cultural awareness and inclusion within our Päiväkoti community. We look forward to seeing the positive impacts of these efforts on student learning and overall well-being.
