 MONTHLY SUMMARY
We had an eventful month, with 533 graduates and 26 cohorts completing their programs. The active student count remains at 515, a testament to our school's commitment to Päiväkoti education. As we bid farewell to some familiar faces, we welcome the new energy of incoming students.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we celebrated the achievements of 29 graduates, including Liam Huang, Huda Abbas, Mia Park, and others who have successfully navigated their chosen paths in Early Childhood Education and Päiväkoti Teacher Training. Their passion and dedication will undoubtedly make a positive impact on future generations.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we enter July, our focus lies in ensuring a smooth transition for new students while maintaining the high standard of education that Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti is known for. Challenges such as managing class sizes and adapting to the diverse needs of our student body remain at the forefront of our concerns. We look forward to another productive month of growth and learning.
