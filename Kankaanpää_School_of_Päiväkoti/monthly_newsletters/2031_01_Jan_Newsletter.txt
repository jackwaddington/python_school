 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This January, our school community has experienced a significant growth and transformation. With 534 active students enrolled across various courses, we've seen a total of 323 graduates and 193 dropouts. The energy and dedication displayed by our students in the field of Päiväkoti education have been truly inspiring.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to our 36 graduates this month! Notable among them are Mehmet Özdemir, who demonstrated exceptional skill in Early Childhood Education, and Ayaan Ali, who showcased remarkable dedication throughout her studies. We wish all our graduates continued success in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD
February promises to be an exciting month as we welcome new students and continue to expand our curriculum offerings. Our challenge lies in ensuring that we maintain the high standards of education that Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti is known for, while accommodating the growing number of learners. We look forward to a productive and rewarding month ahead.
