 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
November was a significant month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti as we welcomed 479 active students, with 196 graduating. Although we saw 125 dropouts this month, our diverse student body continues to thrive in the field of early childhood education.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we congratulate 25 outstanding students who graduated: Emma Cho, Ibrahim Diop, Yuna Nakamura, Hiroshi Tanaka, Sara Yang, Yara Ngosa, Laila Al-Hakim, Lukas Schmidt, Amina Kamara, Raquel Silva, Yara Al-Kilani, Ana Rodrigues, Ramiz Mondal, Oliver O'Sullivan, Léa Lopez, Chloe Kim, Sofia Olsen, Ana Gomes, Ahmed Hassan, Mia Patel, Eva Ocampo, Eva Mendez, Fatima Al-Khalili, and Juan Gonzalez. Their dedication and hard work will undoubtedly make a positive impact in their future Päiväkoti classrooms.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into December, our focus remains on continued growth and development for both our students and faculty. We anticipate challenges ahead with the upcoming holiday season, but we are confident that through teamwork and dedication, we will maintain a supportive and nurturing environment for all. Let's work together to ensure another successful month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti!
