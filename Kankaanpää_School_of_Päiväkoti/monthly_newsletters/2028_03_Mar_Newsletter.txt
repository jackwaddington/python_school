 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
This month has been a period of both growth and change at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 346 active students, we've seen strong engagement across our various courses, particularly in Early Childhood Education, Finnish Language and Culture, Play-Based Learning Strategies, Health and Safety in Päiväkoti, and Päiväkoti Curriculum Development. However, we also experienced a high number of dropouts, totalling 96 students. This highlights the need for ongoing support and encouragement to help our students navigate their studies successfully.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our eight dedicated graduates who completed their courses this month! Aya Matsumoto, Yara Al-Said, Sophia Park, Lila Díaz, Oliver Mendez, Sophia Lee, Sara El-Amin, and Ahmed Hassan have all demonstrated a remarkable commitment to the field of Päiväkoti. We wish them continued success in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into April, our focus will be on fostering a supportive learning environment that addresses the needs of our students, particularly those who may be struggling due to the recent changes. We encourage everyone to embrace the challenges ahead and continue working towards their goals with determination and resilience. Let's make the most of this new month together!
