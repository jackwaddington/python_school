 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
As March comes to a close, we've seen an active participation of 383 students in our Päiväkoti programs. While we have celebrated the successes of 19 graduates, we also acknowledge the departure of 48 students. Our school capacity remains at 40 seats, and we look forward to welcoming new students in April.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we are proud to highlight our Päiväkoti Teacher Training graduates Lina Zhang, Eva Mendez, Leyla Akyol, and Lara Seki who have demonstrated exceptional dedication and passion for Päiväkoti education. We wish them the very best in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we transition into April, we will focus on improving our student retention rates and enhancing our curriculum to better serve our students' needs. We encourage everyone to remain engaged, supportive, and committed to fostering a nurturing and inclusive learning environment at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
