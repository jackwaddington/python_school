 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
November has been a transformative month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with a total of 323 active students and several notable achievements. The school's capacity was stretched as we welcomed 112 new enrollments in Early Childhood Education, 157 in Finnish Language and Culture, and 80 in Päiväkoti Curriculum Development. Despite this, we experienced a high dropout rate of 77 students, which is a concern we must address moving forward.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Regrettably, no graduates were celebrated during November. We eagerly look forward to the successes of our students in the upcoming months.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we enter December, it is crucial that we reevaluate our strategies to address the high dropout rate and ensure a more supportive environment for all our students. We also anticipate a busy month with several unit award ceremonies scheduled, celebrating our students' hard-earned achievements in their respective fields. Let's continue to foster an engaging and nurturing learning community at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
