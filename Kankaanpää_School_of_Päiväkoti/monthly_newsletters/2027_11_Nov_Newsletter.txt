 MONTHLY SUMMARY
November was a challenging but fruitful month for Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 8 graduates, we celebrated the accomplishments of Zara Patel, Aya Al-Husseini, Noura El-Sayed, and Sara Al-Khateeb in our Early Childhood Education program. However, we also saw a high number of dropouts, totaling 36 students, which has put pressure on our resources and capacity. As we move forward, we remain committed to providing a supportive learning environment for all our students.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to Zara Patel, Aya Al-Husseini, Noura El-Sayed, and Sara Al-Khateeb on their successful graduation from Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti's Early Childhood Education program. Each of them brought dedication, creativity, and a passion for children to the classroom, and we are confident they will make significant contributions to the field of education.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we head into December, our focus is on reinforcing our commitment to supporting every student's success. With fewer students enrolled due to the high number of dropouts this month, we have an opportunity to provide more individualized attention and ensure that all remaining students thrive in their educational journey at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. We look forward to a productive and fulfilling December!
