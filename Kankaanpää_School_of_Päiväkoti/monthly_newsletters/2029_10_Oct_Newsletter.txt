 MONTHLY SUMMARY
October at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti was a month of significant growth and accomplishment. With 188 graduates, our school community celebrates their success in the field of early childhood education and Päiväkoti. The school's capacity was stretched but we are proud to have maintained our commitment to quality education for all our students.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we congratulate the following outstanding graduates: Amina Kamara (she/her), Kaori Matsumoto (she/her), Isabella Rodriguez (She/Her), Ricardo Gómez (he/him), and Yara El-Khatib (she/her) who excelled in Child Development in Päiväkoti. Also, kudos to Giorgio Rossi (he/him) and Oliver Williams (he/him) for their exceptional work in Early Childhood Education. We wish them all the best in their future endeavors!

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into November, we anticipate a challenging but exciting month ahead. With our focus on continuous improvement, we will strive to address areas of need and continue to provide the best learning environment for our students. We encourage everyone to embrace the upcoming opportunities with enthusiasm and determination. Let's make the most of it!
