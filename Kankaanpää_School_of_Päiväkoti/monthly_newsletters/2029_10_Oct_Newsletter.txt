 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
October at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti has been a month of significant growth and change. With 40 students receiving unit awards, it's clear that our students are thriving in their respective fields. However, we also experienced a high number of dropouts, which is concerning and requires our attention moving forward. As a school dedicated to Early Childhood Education and beyond, we remain committed to nurturing the potential within each student while fostering an environment that supports their personal growth.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we congratulate 90 graduates who have completed their courses with us. Among them are Zara Lee (she/her) from Finnish Language and Culture, Lukas Olsen (he/him) from Health and Safety in Päiväkoti, Ingrid Santos (she/her) from Child Psychology in Päiväkoti, Mia Chang (she/her) from Finnish Language and Culture, and many more. We wish them the best of luck in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we enter November, our focus will be on addressing the challenges faced this month and working towards improving student retention. We encourage all students to embrace their learning journey, seek support when needed, and continue striving for excellence. Let's make November a month of growth and success at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
