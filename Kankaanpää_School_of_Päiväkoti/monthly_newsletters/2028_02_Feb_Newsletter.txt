 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This February has been a rollercoaster for our Päiväkoti community, with an enrollment of 356 students and a significant number of dropouts totaling 91. Despite these challenges, we have seen some outstanding performances, particularly in the Unit Award Ceremony where 34 students were recognized for their hard work and dedication to their courses. Our school's mission continues to thrive, fostering an environment that encourages growth and development in early childhood education, language and culture, curriculum development, child psychology, play-based learning strategies, administration, art, and safety.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
We proudly congratulate three graduates this month – Lucas Mendes, Fatima Benzakour, and Juan Rodriguez – for their successful completion of the Child Psychology in Päiväkoti course. Each of them demonstrated remarkable commitment, resilience, and a genuine passion for fostering positive development among young children. We are confident that they will make significant contributions to our field.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into March, we are faced with the challenge of filling 91 empty seats left by our recent dropouts while ensuring that the quality of education remains high. We encourage everyone within our community to continue striving for excellence and supporting each other on this journey. Let's embrace the new month with renewed determination and passion for nurturing the minds of future generations!
