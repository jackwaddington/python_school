 MONTHLY SUMMARY
February has been a productive month at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti, with 16 graduates and 46 dropouts. While it's unfortunate to see some students leave our community, we are proud of those who have completed their courses and moved on to their next steps in the field of Early Childhood Education. The current enrollment numbers reflect a vibrant and engaged student body, eager to learn and grow in the nurturing environment of Päiväkoti.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into March, we are focused on providing a supportive environment for our remaining students as they continue their journey towards graduation. We recognize that some courses have minimal enrollment, such as Managing Challenging Behaviors in Päiväkoti and Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education, and we will be working to address this issue in the coming months. We are also looking forward to welcoming new students in our upcoming cohorts. Let's continue to support each other and strive for excellence in all that we do at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
