EXAM: Päiväkoti Curriculum Development
────────────────────────────────────────

Q1: Which early childhood education philosophy emphasizes child-led, collaborative, and project-based learning?
  A) Reggio Emilia Approach
  B) Montessori Method
  C) HighScope Approach
Answer: A

Q2: In Developmentally Appropriate Practice (DAP), educators consider the interests and learning styles of each child when planning lessons. True or False?
  A) True
  B) False
Answer: A

Q3: Child-initiated and adult-supported play encourages learning through play experiences while promoting social, emotional, cognitive, and physical development. How does this approach work?
  A) Educators dictate the direction of the activity and provide suggestions for learning
  B) Educators observe and engage in play with children, offering questions and materials to support their learning
  C) Children are left to play without adult interaction
Answer: B

Q4: Emergent curriculum is based on the interests, questions, and emerging needs of the children in a particular classroom. What does this approach do?
  A) Implements a fixed, pre-planned curriculum without considering the children's interests
  B) Creates a flexible, responsive, and child-centered curriculum that evolves based on the children's interests
  C) Focuses only on the teacher's interests and ignores the children's questions
Answer: B
