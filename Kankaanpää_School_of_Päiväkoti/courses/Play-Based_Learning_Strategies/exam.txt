Q1: What is free play in the context of Play-Based Learning Strategies in Päiväkoti (Finnish Daycare)?
  A) A structured, adult-led learning activity with predefined goals.
  B) An unstructured, self-directed learning activity where children are allowed to explore and interact with their environment freely.
  C) A type of play where children act out scenarios pretending to be adults or characters from stories.
  D) A sensory play involving activities that stimulate a child's senses.
Answer: B

Q2: What is one advantage of block play in the context of Play-Based Learning Strategies in Päiväkoti (Finnish Daycare)?
  A) It promotes social interaction and collaboration.
  B) It helps children develop their senses, motor skills, and understanding of the world around them.
  C) It encourages creativity and imagination but does not promote spatial reasoning or problem-solving skills.
  D) It develops language skills, empathy, and understanding of social roles and relationships.
