EXAM: Play-Based Learning Strategies
────────────────────────────────────────

Q1: Which strategy in Finnish early childhood education emphasizes the educational potential of play?
  A) Child-Directed Learning
  B) Structured Play
  C) Play as Learning
  D) Loose Parts Play
Answer: C

Q2: Which type of play provides opportunities for children to express themselves creatively and develop problem-solving skills?
  A) Structured Play
  B) Teacher-Directed Learning
  C) Open-Ended Play
  D) Loose Parts Play
Answer: C
