 Title: Art and Creative Activities for Children in Päiväkoti

1. **Art Education**
   - Definition: A systematic approach to teaching art, emphasizing learning through experience, expression, imagination, and creativity.
   - Function: To develop children's cognitive, emotional, social, and physical skills.
   - Example: Using materials like paints, crayons, and clay to create self-portraits or nature-inspired sculptures.

2. **Creative Dramatics**
   - Definition: A method of teaching theater arts that encourages children's imagination, creativity, and self-expression through dramatic play and storytelling.
   - Function: To develop language skills, improvisation, empathy, and social interaction.
   - Example: Acting out a favorite fairy tale with props and costumes, or creating a simple play based on a group discussion about their day.

3. **Visual Arts**
   - Definition: The creation of works through the use of sight and imagination, often including drawing, painting, sculpture, printmaking, and photography.
   - Function: To enhance children's ability to observe, imagine, and express themselves visually.
   - Example: Creating a collage from magazine cutouts, or designing a poster about an animal they have learned about in Päiväkoti.

4. **Music and Movement**
   - Definition: Activities that combine music with physical movement to stimulate rhythm, coordination, and self-expression.
   - Function: To develop children's sense of rhythm, motor skills, and emotional expression.
   - Example: Dancing to a favorite song, or creating a rhythm using different household items like pots and spoons.
