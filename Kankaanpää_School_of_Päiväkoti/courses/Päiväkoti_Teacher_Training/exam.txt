Q1: What is Early Childhood Education according to the Päiväkoti Teacher Training Study Material?
  A) A systematic process that equips individuals with the knowledge, skills, and attitudes necessary to nurture and educate children from birth to eight years of age in a day care center or kindergarten setting.
  B) The study of how children grow and develop emotionally, physically, and intellectually from conception through adolescence.
  C) The process of deciding and organizing the content, objectives, methods, materials, schedule, and evaluation for a teaching program.
  D) The scientific study of the mental processes of children, their behavior, and emotional development.
Answer: A

Q2: Which area does curriculum planning in Päiväkoti primarily focus on?
  A) Understanding child development.
  B) The study of the mental processes of children, their behavior, and emotional development.
  C) Deciding and organizing the content, objectives, methods, materials, schedule, and evaluation for a teaching program.
  D) Creating a safe, stimulating, and nurturing environment for children's holistic growth.
Answer: C
