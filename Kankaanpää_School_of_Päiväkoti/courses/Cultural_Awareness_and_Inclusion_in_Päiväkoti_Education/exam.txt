EXAM: Cultural Awareness and Inclusion in Päiväkoti Education
────────────────────────────────────────

Q1: What is the definition of Cultural Awareness?
  A) Recognition and understanding of cultural differences and similarities among people within and across societies.
  B) The ability to cross cultural boundaries effectively and appropriately, interacting effectively with people of different cultures, adapting to cultural differences while preserving one's own culture.
  C) An approach to education where diversity is valued, and every student is provided with equal opportunities to learn and participate in the learning process.
  D) The active inviting, welcoming, and valuing of diverse individuals and their cultural identities, and making an intentional effort to include these perspectives in all aspects of the Päiväkoti environment.
Answer: A

Q2: Which of the following is NOT a function of Inclusive Education?
  A) Promotes social cohesion.
  B) Encourages active participation of all students.
  C) Enhances academic achievement for all.
  D) Fosters understanding, empathy, and collaboration among students.
Answer: D
