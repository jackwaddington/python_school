 THIS WEEK
It's been an eventful week here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 444 active students, our classrooms are buzzing with energy and learning. While we bid farewell to no graduates this week, we've said goodbye to a total of 40 over the past year, a number that reminds us of the journey each student embarks on. We also experienced some setbacks, as 66 students decided to part ways with us – a loss we will work diligently to mitigate moving forward. On a positive note, our dedicated educators completed 20 units this week!

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students,

This week was filled with ups and downs. We've seen progress in our academic achievements and the dedication of our faculty, but we've also felt the absence of those who have left us. Let's continue to nurture a supportive environment where each individual can flourish, grow, and learn. Together, we will make Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti even stronger.
