It's been a week of mixed emotions here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 3 more graduates joining our alumni, we celebrate their success and wish them all the best in their future endeavors. Simultaneously, we mourn the loss of 243 students who decided to discontinue their studies with us. It's a bittersweet moment, but we remain optimistic as we continue to nurture the remaining 437 students.
