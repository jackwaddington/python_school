 THIS WEEK
We welcomed 512 active students into our classrooms, each one bringing their unique spirit and energy. It's heartening to see the momentum building as we've graduated three more Päiväkoti pioneers, bringing our total to 271. However, it is with regret that we've also experienced dropouts from 167 students this week - a setback we aim to address and learn from.

UNIT COMPLETIONS reached 19, showcasing the dedication of our students as they progress through their courses. It was a rewarding moment to see 81 students recognized for their hard work at the Unit Award Ceremony this week - congratulations to Taro Yamada, Maximilian Silva, Sara Al-Khalidi, and all the others!

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear Kankaanpää family, let's celebrate our victories while acknowledging our challenges. We are on a journey together, and every step we take brings us closer to fostering the next generation of Päiväkoti educators.

NEXT WEEK
The Unit Award Ceremony has been a fantastic reminder of our students' achievements. In eight weeks, we'll gather once more for the Graduation Ceremony, honoring those who have completed their courses and embarked on their journey as Päiväkoti professionals.
