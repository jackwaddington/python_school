 THIS WEEK
It's been a rollercoaster week at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. We bid farewell to 6 graduates, who have made their mark in our community and set off on exciting new paths. Simultaneously, we've had to say goodbye to 35 students who decided to pursue other opportunities, a loss we feel deeply. Despite this, we are heartened by the progress of 16 successful unit completions and the continued dedication of our 359 active students.
