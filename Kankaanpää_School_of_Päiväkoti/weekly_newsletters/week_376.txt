 THIS WEEK
It's been a week of mixed emotions here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. We welcomed four new graduates into the alumni community, yet we also had to bid farewell to 284 students who decided to discontinue their studies. The Unit Award Ceremony was a heartening event, where 59 students received awards across various courses, and I must say it's truly inspiring to see such dedication and hard work being recognized.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students, I would like to express my sincere congratulations to our new graduates and a warm welcome to those who joined us this week. To those leaving us, we thank you for your contributions and wish you the very best in your future endeavors. As for the rest of us, let's continue to support each other, work hard, and strive for excellence.

NEXT WEEK
