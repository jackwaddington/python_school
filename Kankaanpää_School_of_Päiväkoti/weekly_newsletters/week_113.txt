 THIS WEEK
We've seen our student body shrink this week, with 93 students deciding to leave and only two new graduates joining the ranks. The emptiness left by the departures is hard to ignore, but the smiles on Emilie Nguyen's and Elias Park's faces at their graduation are a poignant reminder of the opportunities that await those who choose Päiväkoti. We completed eight unit courses this week, which brings us closer to our goals.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
To my dear staff and students, it's been a rollercoaster week. I feel the weight of the losses, but I am heartened by the progress we've made. Let's remember Emilie and Elias as they embark on new journeys. Here's to their success and ours!
