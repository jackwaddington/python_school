 THIS WEEK
We've had quite the mix of emotions this week. It's bittersweet to see 2 more of our dedicated students graduate, but it's a testament to the quality of education we provide that they're moving on with their dreams fulfilled. At the same time, we bid farewell to 221 students who left mid-term, a number we hope will decrease in the future. Despite this, we are proud to have added 66 new unit award recipients to our roster this week!

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students, our collective efforts continue to bear fruit. Let's take pride in the 20 completed units this week, but let's also reflect on those who left us. We must remember that every departure is an opportunity for growth and improvement. As we prepare for next week's Unit Award Ceremony, let's celebrate our achievements together while keeping a sharp focus on our goals.

NEXT WEEK
Next week, we gather to honor the hard work of 66 students at the Unit Award Ceremony. Let's come together and applaud their successes. It's a moment for us all to share in their joy and be inspired by their dedication.
