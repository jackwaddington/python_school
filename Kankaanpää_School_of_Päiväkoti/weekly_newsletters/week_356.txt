 THIS WEEK
What a whirlwind week it's been! We welcomed 2 more graduates into the world of Päiväkoti education, while saying goodbye to 68 students who chose a different path. The sheer determination and resilience displayed by our students never ceases to amaze me. We also saw impressive progress with 17 unit completions across various courses.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
To our graduates, congratulations on your achievements! To those still learning with us, keep pushing through and remember that every challenge is an opportunity for growth. And to our departing students, thank you for the energy, creativity, and laughter you brought to Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. We wish you all the best in your future endeavors.

NEXT WEEK
