 THIS WEEK
It's been a week filled with mixed emotions. While we've seen our active student count maintain at 408, it's disheartening to note that this week marks another 235 dropouts since the school's inception. On a positive note, our students continue to excel, completing 12 units and receiving 49 awards across various courses.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students, I hope this newsletter finds you well. This week has been challenging, but it's also been a reminder of the resilience and dedication that permeates our school. To those who have faced difficulties, know that we are here to support you. To those who have excelled, congratulations on your achievements. Let's continue to strive for excellence together.
