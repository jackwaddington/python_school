The energy here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti has been palpable, with 436 active students bustling about, engaged in their studies. Dear staff and students, this week was filled with a mix of triumphs and setbacks. Let's celebrate our successes, such as the 11 unit completions and the dedication of our 43 new award recipients. Let us remember that each student who leaves is an opportunity to grow and learn from the reasons behind their departure, so we can better support those still with us.
