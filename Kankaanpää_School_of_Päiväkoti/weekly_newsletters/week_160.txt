 THIS WEEK
We've seen some significant milestones this week as we welcomed two new graduates into the world of Päiväkoti education, and 56 students received unit awards. Our active student count stands at a robust 474, which is both heartening and challenging in equal measure. The empty seats remind us of those we've lost, but the full ones give us hope for the future.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear Staff and Students,
I want to express my gratitude for your unwavering dedication this week. Your hard work and determination have resulted in the successes we are celebrating today. However, I must also acknowledge our losses and remind us that every empty seat is a reminder of the potential we must strive to fulfill. Let's continue to support each other as we navigate these joys and challenges together.

NEXT WEEK
