 THIS WEEK
It's been an eventful week here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. We welcomed three new graduates into the world, while saying goodbye to 201 students who had to leave our community. The hard work and dedication shown by both graduating and remaining students was evident in the completion of 9 units and the achievement of unit awards for 40 students.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students, it's been a week filled with both joy and sorrow. I am proud of our graduates and the strides they have made, but we also feel the loss of those who had to leave. Let's continue supporting each other as we strive for growth and success in our Päiväkoti community.

NEXT WEEK
Next week, we will be holding two ceremonies: the Unit Award Ceremony and the Graduation Ceremony. Both events are opportunities to celebrate the hard work and achievements of our students and honor their dedication to their studies at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.
