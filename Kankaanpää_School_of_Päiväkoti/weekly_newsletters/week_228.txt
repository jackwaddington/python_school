 THIS WEEK
We've seen progress this week with 72 students receiving unit awards and the graduation of 23 students. Yet, we also bid farewell to 152 dropouts, a loss we can't ignore. The spirit here remains high, but we know there's room for improvement.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students, this week has been a mix of joy and sorrow. We celebrate the achievements of our graduates and award recipients while acknowledging the challenges faced by those who have chosen to leave. Let's continue supporting each other as we strive for excellence in Päiväkoti education.

NEXT WEEK
