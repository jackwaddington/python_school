 THIS WEEK
We've seen another week of dedicated learning and growth here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. With 73 students receiving unit awards, it's clear our community is thriving. We're proud to announce one new graduate, Liam Lee, who has completed his journey with us. However, we also mourn the loss of 156 students who have chosen to leave, and hope they find the same passion for Päiväkoti education elsewhere.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students, a big congratulations to our new graduate, Liam Lee! We're also grateful for each of our 496 active students who continue to make Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti their home. To those who have chosen to leave, we wish you all the best in your future endeavors. Let's keep pushing forward together.

NEXT WEEK
