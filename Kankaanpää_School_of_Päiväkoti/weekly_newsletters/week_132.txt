 THIS WEEK
What a rollercoaster week it's been! We welcomed one new graduate into the world, Ana Gomez, while saying a bittersweet goodbye to 31 others. Amidst these changes, we also had to deal with some tough news - a record 60 students deciding to part ways with us. On the bright side, our dedicated team has worked tirelessly and we've seen an impressive 14 unit completions across various courses.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
To those graduating, I offer my heartfelt congratulations. To those leaving, I wish you all the best in your future endeavors. And to our remaining students and staff, keep up the excellent work. The progress we've made this week is a testament to your hard work and dedication. Let's continue to support each other as we navigate through both triumphs and challenges.

NEXT WEEK
Mark your calendars for two important events: the Unit Award Ceremony, where 61 students will be honored for their achievements, and the Graduation Ceremony, where we will celebrate the accomplishments of 8 more graduates. Let's gather together to acknowledge our successes and look forward to new beginnings.
