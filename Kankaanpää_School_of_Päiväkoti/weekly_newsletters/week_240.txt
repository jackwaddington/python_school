 THIS WEEK
The halls of Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti echo with the energy of progress and resilience. This week, we've welcomed 525 active students, a figure that speaks volumes about our commitment to early childhood education. However, it's a bittersweet moment as we bid farewell to 22 graduates and mourn the loss of 165 dropouts. The silence left by their departure is a stark reminder of the challenges we face in nurturing our future educators.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear colleagues, let us celebrate the achievements of our 26 unit completions and 22 graduates this week. Yet, it's crucial to acknowledge that these victories come with a heavier heart due to the losses we've endured. Our resilience will be tested, but remember: every student who walks through our doors carries immense potential, and we are here to ensure they reach their fullest.

NEXT WEEK
The Unit Award Ceremony and Graduation Ceremony are just around the corner. Let us gather together in unity and honor these remarkable moments, as we recognize our students' hard-earned successes and send them forth with our utmost support and admiration.
