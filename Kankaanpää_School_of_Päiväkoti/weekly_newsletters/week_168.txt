What a week! With 448 active students and 1 new graduate, Sara El-Zeiny, we're excited to see our community growing. Unfortunately, we also had 94 dropouts this week, a loss we deeply feel. Dear staff and students,
The loss of some of our students is a heavy blow, but their time with us was not in vain. We will remember them fondly and continue to support those who remain. Congratulations to all the students who received unit awards this week! Your dedication to learning shines bright. Let's continue to inspire each other as we move forward.
