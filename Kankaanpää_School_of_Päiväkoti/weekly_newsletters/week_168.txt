 THIS WEEK
We've seen a mix of progress and change this week. With 367 active students, we're keeping the Päiväkoti spirit alive. The graduation of 3 esteemed students marks a significant milestone, yet it's tinged with a touch of melancholy as we bid them farewell. We've also had to let go of 229 students who found other paths, a loss that we feel deeply. But the completion of 10 units and the Unit Award Ceremony this week are reminders of our ongoing successes.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students,
The graduation of Oliver Nguyen, Yannick Mbenga, and Ibrahim Oluwatoyin is a testament to their hard work and dedication. Simultaneously, we mourn the loss of 229 students who've decided to leave our community. Let's take this moment to reflect on what we've achieved together this week and look forward to more milestones ahead.

NEXT WEEK
This week, we'll be hosting both the Unit Award Ceremony and the Graduation Ceremony. These events are a chance for us to celebrate our students' achievements and wish them well in their future endeavors. Let's make these ceremonies a memorable occasion for all involved!
