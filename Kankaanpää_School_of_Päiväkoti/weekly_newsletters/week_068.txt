 THIS WEEK
It's been quite an eventful week here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. Despite seeing a dip in our active student count, we've managed to maintain our momentum with six unit completions and the graduation of no less than 34 students at our upcoming Unit Award Ceremony. The empty Health and Safety in Päiväkoti course and the decline in Art and Creative Activities for Children enrollment have left us reflecting on ways to revitalize these offerings.

Dear staff and students, I want to acknowledge our losses this week, but also express my pride in our graduating students. Their accomplishments are a testament to your hard work and dedication. Let's continue supporting each other as we navigate through challenges and celebrate successes alike.
