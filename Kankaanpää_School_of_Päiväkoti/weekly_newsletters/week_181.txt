 THIS WEEK
As the week comes to an end, we find ourselves with 394 active students in our care, a number that is constantly evolving. This week marked the graduation of three more students – Ryuichi Tanaka, Leo Lee, and Zara Wang – adding to the 62 proud graduates we've seen this academic year. On the flip side, we also bid farewell to 241 students who decided to move on, a sobering reminder of the transient nature of our community.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
To all our students and staff – a heartfelt congratulations to our new graduates, and a warm welcome to those who have recently joined us. To those who are leaving, we thank you for your contributions and wish you the very best in your future endeavors. Let's remember the value of each other as we continue our journey together.

NEXT WEEK
In three weeks, we will gather for the Unit Award Ceremony, an opportunity to recognize and celebrate the achievements of our students across various units. We look forward to this event, a testament to the hard work and dedication of our entire community.
