 THIS WEEK
We welcomed four new graduates into the world of Päiväkoti education, a testament to our dedicated team and the students' hard work. Yet, we also faced losses with 277 dropouts this week, a number that leaves us with a heavy heart. The Unit Award Ceremony recognized 81 students for their outstanding achievements in various courses, shining light amidst the shadows of setbacks.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
To our graduates, congratulations on your well-deserved success. To those who've chosen to leave us, we wish you luck and hope that one day you'll return to Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti with a renewed passion for teaching and learning. As we prepare for the upcoming Unit Award Ceremony, let's celebrate our victories while keeping in mind the challenges ahead.

NEXT WEEK
The Unit Award Ceremony will be held this week, honoring students from Early Childhood Education, Päiväkoti Teacher Training, Infant and Toddler Care, Play-Based Learning Strategies, Child Development in Päiväkoti, and Päiväkoti Curriculum Design. Let's come together to acknowledge their efforts and inspire them for future achievements.
