 THIS WEEK
What a whirlwind of emotions! We've seen 77 students shine in the Unit Award Ceremony, and next week, we'll celebrate another milestone with 34 graduates. It's been an honor to witness their growth and achievements. However, it's also a reminder that we've lost 214 students along the way – a loss felt deeply here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
To our graduates: You have worked hard to get where you are, and I couldn't be prouder. To those who left us this week, know that we will carry your spirit with us always. Let's continue to support each other through the ups and downs, and remember – every challenge is an opportunity for growth.

NEXT WEEK
Next week, we gather to honor our graduates at their ceremonies. As we celebrate their successes, let's also remember the journeys that led us here and look forward to those yet to come. Let's make it a week of joy, reflection, and hope for the future.
