We've had another week filled with the vibrant energy of our students and the dedication of our staff. It's a bittersweet feeling to see 354 active students, knowing that we've also bid farewell to 2 more graduates, bringing our total graduates this year to 8. Let's continue to nurture their growth and strive for excellence together.
