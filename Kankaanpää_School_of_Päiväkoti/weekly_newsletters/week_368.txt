 THIS WEEK
What a week! With 539 students actively learning and growing, it's clear our passion for early education continues to flourish. It's always bittersweet when we see graduates like Isabella García and Sophia Lee move on, but the knowledge they carry with them into the world is an incredible testament to our mission. We also celebrated 60 students receiving unit awards - congratulations to each of you! However, it's worth noting that 279 dropouts have left us this year. Let's continue supporting one another as we navigate these challenges together.
