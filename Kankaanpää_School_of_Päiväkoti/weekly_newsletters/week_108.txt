 THIS WEEK
It's been a week of mixed emotions here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. While we bid farewell to 3 graduates, we also had to say goodbye to 87 students who chose to leave our community mid-course. However, the light at the end of the tunnel shines brightly as 29 students received unit awards, a testament to their hard work and dedication.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students, this week has been challenging, but your resilience never ceases to amaze me. I want to express my deepest gratitude to those who are staying with us, and my sincere well-wishes to our departing students. Let's gather our strength for the upcoming ceremonies and continue to support each other on this educational journey.

NEXT WEEK
Join us for two significant events: the Unit Award Ceremony and the Graduation Ceremony. These celebrations will be a testament to the perseverance, growth, and achievements of our students in the face of adversity. Let's celebrate together!
