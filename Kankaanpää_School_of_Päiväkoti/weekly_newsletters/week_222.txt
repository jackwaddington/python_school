 THIS WEEK
We've bid farewell to another four students this week, who have successfully completed their Päiväkoti education. It's always a bittersweet moment when our students graduate, but we are proud of their achievements and excited for what lies ahead for them. As we continue to nurture the remaining 527 active students in our care, we also mourn the loss of 144 students who have chosen to discontinue their education with us. We hope that they find the right path for their learning journey elsewhere.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students, I am delighted to see that we've managed to complete 13 units this week, and our graduation count has now reached an impressive 229. However, it is with a heavy heart that I acknowledge the 144 students who have left us mid-course. Let's support each other as we continue to create an environment of growth, learning, and inclusivity for all.
