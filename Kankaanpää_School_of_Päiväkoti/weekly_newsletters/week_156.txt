 THIS WEEK
The holiday season has been particularly festive here at Kankaanpää School of Päiväkoti. As we near the end of this term, I'm proud to report that we've seen a steady increase in unit completions and graduations, with a total of 5 new graduates joining our esteemed alumni network. However, it's also worth noting that we've had an unfortunate rise in dropouts – let's all work together to ensure no student slips through the cracks during this busy season.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL
Dear staff and students,
The spirit of giving has never been more palpable than it is now, with so many of you reaching milestones in your Päiväkoti journey. To those graduating, I wish you nothing but success and joy as you embark on new chapters. To our departing colleagues, know that you will be missed, and we hope to see you again soon. Let's keep each other motivated and supported as we approach the end of the year.

NEXT WEEK
