 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
January has proven to be a productive and challenging month at the London School of Critical Thinking. With 64 graduates, we've witnessed the culmination of hard work and dedication from our students in various courses such as Philosophical Inquiry, Logic and Reasoning, and Critical Thinking Strategies. However, we also bid farewell to 22 students who decided to pursue other paths. As we reflect on this dynamic month, we remain committed to fostering critical thinking and encouraging intellectual curiosity in our field.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our outstanding graduates, including Jane Thompson from the Philosophical Inquiry course and Thomas Williams from Logic and Reasoning. Their achievements serve as an inspiration to all of us at the London School of Critical Thinking.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we transition into February, we face a slightly smaller cohort with 4 active groups. Our focus for next month will be on maintaining our high standards and providing unparalleled support to each student, ensuring they reach their full potential in the pursuit of critical thinking excellence. We look forward to another month of growth and learning at the London School of Critical Thinking.
