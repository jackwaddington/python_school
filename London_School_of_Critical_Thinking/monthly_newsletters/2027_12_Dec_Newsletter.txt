This past month has been a productive one for our community, with a total of 34 students graduating and our active student count remaining strong at 17. Despite the nine dropouts, we are proud to continue fostering critical thinking within our field. The diverse minds that make up the London School of Critical Thinking continue to challenge, inspire, and learn from each other in the pursuit of understanding and reason.

We celebrate two exceptional graduates this month: Sophia Ocampo (she/her) and Omar Ben-Youssef (he/him). Their success is a testament to their dedication and the potential of our school to shape the critical thinkers of tomorrow.

Let's continue pushing the boundaries of understanding together!
