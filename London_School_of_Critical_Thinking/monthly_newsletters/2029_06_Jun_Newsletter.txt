 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
The month of June has been a remarkable display of intellectual growth and resilience. We welcomed 23 active students, graduated an impressive 75, and saw 22 students complete their journey with us. The dedication shown by our learners is a testament to the transformative power of Critical Thinking in today's ever-evolving world.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month we bid farewell to 10 exceptional individuals who have successfully navigated the complexities of critical thinking, demonstrating their potential as future leaders in our field. Among them, Lina Zhou, Mariam Nguyen, James Park, Lukas Schmidt, Sophie Diaz, Liam Li, Yara Gómez, Oliver Gonzalez, Aisha Khan, and Sofia Li. Their accomplishments serve as a beacon of hope for the future of Critical Thinking.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we step into July, our focus will be on maintaining our high standards while seeking to expand our capacity to accommodate the growing interest in critical thinking education. We anticipate challenges ahead but remain steadfast in our commitment to nurturing a new generation of thinkers who can tackle the complexities of tomorrow.
