August was a productive and rewarding month at London School of Critical Thinking. With a total of 28 graduates, the school's impact on critical thinking is evident. Though we saw seven dropouts, our active student count remains strong at 25. The enthusiasm in our classrooms, as evidenced by our three well-attended courses, was palpable, reflecting a shared dedication to the pursuit of critical thinking skills.
