 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
August was a vibrant month at the London School of Critical Thinking, with 81 graduates marking a significant milestone. Although we bid farewell to 23 students who left our institution, it's important to remember that each departure allows us space to welcome fresh minds. Our active student count remains steady at 16, and the diversity of our cohorts continues to enrich the critical discourse within our community.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to Lukas O'Sullivan (Philosophical Inquiry), Nina Wang (Logic and Reasoning), Yusuf Patel (Logic and Reasoning), Leonardo Dos Santos (Philosophical Inquiry), and Oliver Lopez (Logic and Reasoning) for their outstanding achievements. Their contributions to the intellectual landscape at our school will be greatly missed but their impact will continue to inspire future generations of critical thinkers.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into September, it's crucial that we focus on maintaining our strong student-teacher interactions and supporting those who are new to our community. We also aim to address the challenges associated with our school capacity by ensuring that our enrollment strategies are effective in meeting our students' needs while maintaining a high standard of critical thinking instruction.
