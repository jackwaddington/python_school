 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This July has been a productive and enriching month at the London School of Critical Thinking. With a total of 25 active students, we've seen an impressive graduating class of 28, demonstrating our commitment to fostering critical thinkers in various fields. Unfortunately, we also bid farewell to seven dedicated individuals who found it necessary to pursue other paths. As always, we remain dedicated to our mission and continue to strive for excellence in the realm of philosophical discourse and logical reasoning.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to six outstanding students who received unit awards this month: Emily O'Sullivan, Ramesh Patel, Maria Garcia, Sophia Ocampo, Daniel Park, and Zara Yang. Their hard work and commitment to critical thinking have set them apart in our community, and we are confident that their skills will continue to shine in whatever endeavors they choose to pursue.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into August, we anticipate the arrival of two new cohorts, bringing renewed energy and ideas to our school. We look forward to welcoming these students and continuing to provide them with the tools and knowledge needed for success in critical thinking. With increased capacity, we will also strive to improve our services and support systems, ensuring that every student feels valued and supported on their journey towards becoming a skilled critical thinker.
