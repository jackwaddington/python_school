 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
March has been a remarkable month at the London School of Critical Thinking, with an impressive 37 graduates and 44 active students. Although we experienced a slight decrease in school capacity due to dropouts, our commitment to critical thinking remains unwavering. This month's achievements further solidify our reputation as a leading institution in fostering independent thinkers within the intellectual community.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into April, our focus lies on maintaining the high standards of education that our school is known for. We anticipate continued growth and are eager to welcome new students who share our passion for critical thinking. The challenges ahead will be numerous, but with the support of our community, we are confident in our ability to meet them head-on. Let us continue to cultivate a generation of independent thinkers!
