 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
October was a significant month for the London School of Critical Thinking. With 91 graduates and only 23 dropouts, it demonstrates our students' commitment to honing their critical thinking skills. Although we had fewer active students this month (6), our graduating cohort's impressive achievements underscore the school's dedication to fostering critical thinkers in various fields.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to three outstanding graduates from October: Leonardo Dos Santos, Lukas O'Sullivan, and Mohammad Al-Said. Their success in Logic and Reasoning is a testament to their hard work and the effectiveness of our program.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into November, we must focus on expanding enrollment for all courses, particularly Philosophical Inquiry and Critical Thinking Strategies, which currently have lower student numbers. Filling the remaining 10 seats in our school capacity will help us continue to make a significant impact on critical thinking education. Let's welcome the new month with renewed energy and determination!
