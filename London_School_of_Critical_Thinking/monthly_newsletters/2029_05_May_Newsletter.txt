 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
May has been a productive month at the London School of Critical Thinking, with a total of 70 graduates joining our alumni community. While we saw a significant number of dropouts, it's important to remember that critical thinking is a challenging journey. Our school capacity remains at 16 seats, ready to welcome more students in the upcoming cohorts.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our distinguished graduates Lucas Ng, Sara Dos Santos, Isabella Garcia, Omar El-Khateeb, Winnie Li, Sophia Li, Yara El-Sayed, Nina Wang, Aisha Kumar, Mohammed Ben Salem, and Yusuf Patel. Their dedication to the pursuit of critical thinking has been inspiring, and we are confident they will continue to make valuable contributions in their respective fields.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we enter June, our focus will be on enhancing course offerings and engaging our current students to ensure their continued success. We anticipate the upcoming cohorts to be challenging but promising, and we are committed to fostering an environment conducive to critical thinking and personal growth. Let's continue to question, challenge, and grow together!
