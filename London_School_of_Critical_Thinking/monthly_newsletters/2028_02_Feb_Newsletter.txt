The month of February has been a productive one for us at London School of Critical Thinking. With 44 active students, 37 graduates, and only 9 dropouts, our commitment to fostering critical thinking is clearly paying off. The lively exchange of ideas within our school community, particularly in our core courses, has set the stage for a promising future in the field of philosophy.
