 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
July has been a productive month for the London School of Critical Thinking, with a total of 48 graduates and 23 active students currently enrolled. This reflects our commitment to fostering critical thinking and promoting intellectual growth in our community. We are particularly proud of the success of our Logic and Reasoning course, which saw an impressive number of unit awards this month.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Congratulations to our 10 graduates who successfully completed their programs this July: Elias Sousa, Raj Patel, Nour Al-Said, Sofia Diaz, Mira Choi, Emma Lee, Nina Patel, Aisha Khan, Sebastian Nguyen, and Yuki Nakamura. Their dedication and hard work have not only benefited their personal growth but also enriched our intellectual discourse. We wish them all the best in their future endeavors.

LOOKING AHEAD:
As we move into August, we will focus on maintaining our high standards of teaching while finding ways to accommodate the growing demand for critical thinking skills in today's complex world. Challenges lie ahead, but with the continued support of our students and faculty, we are confident that the London School of Critical Thinking will continue to thrive in fostering critical thinkers who can navigate the uncertainties of the future.
