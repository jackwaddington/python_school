 MONTHLY SUMMARY
We've wrapped up an engaging January at the London School of Critical Thinking, with active students totaling 45 and a steady graduating class of 36. While we bid farewell to nine dedicated learners, it's heartening to see our school's capacity being maximized. The diverse cohorts have made this month rich in intellectual discourse, especially within Philosophical Inquiry, Critical Thinking Strategies, and Logic and Reasoning courses.

LOOKING AHEAD
With a full roster of students returning and eager newcomers on the horizon, February promises to be another exciting month at our school. We anticipate challenges ahead as we strive to maintain our high standards while accommodating an increasing student body. However, with the dedication of our faculty and the continued support of our community, we are confident in our ability to foster critical thinkers who will shape the world for the better.
