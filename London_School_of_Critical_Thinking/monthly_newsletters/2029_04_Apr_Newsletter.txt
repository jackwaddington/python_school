 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
April was a significant month for the London School of Critical Thinking, with 66 graduates joining our growing community. Our active student count remained robust at 32, indicating a steadfast commitment to critical thinking education. As we celebrate these achievements, it's crucial to acknowledge that our capacity for new students is limited due to the current cohorts reaching full enrollment.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
Among our esteemed graduates this month were Sofia Li, James Park, Sara Dos Santos, Yara Gómez, Lukas Schmidt, Hassan Ahmed, Aisha Khan, Juan Rodriguez, Oliver Gonzalez, Amina Khan, Sophie Diaz, Emma Patel, Zara Lee, Lukas Müller, and Anais Patel. Their dedication to critical thinking and intellectual growth sets a high standard for our current students and future graduates alike.

LOOKING AHEAD:
Next month, we will continue to prioritize providing high-quality critical thinking education with an emphasis on increasing school capacity to accommodate more students eager to develop their critical thinking skills. Our focus remains on fostering a vibrant learning community where dialogue, debate, and intellectual growth are at the forefront of all our endeavors. We look forward to welcoming new students and continuing to nurture the success of those already enrolled.
