We have concluded a busy and productive September at the London School of Critical Thinking. Our active student body has remained steady, with 14 engaged learners delving into philosophical inquiries, logic, and reasoning. We are proud to announce the graduation of 57 students this month, further cementing our reputation as a leading institution for critical thinking education. As always, we strive to foster an intellectually vibrant environment, and our school community has not disappointed.

This month's graduates include Luis González (he/him), Yara O'Sullivan (she/her), and Sofia Li (she/her).
