 MONTHLY SUMMARY
We have seen a productive and fruitful month at the London School of Critical Thinking, with our active student count remaining steady at 25. This indicates a strong engagement from our students in their pursuit of critical thinking skills. With an impressive total of 28 graduates this month, we are proud to be fostering a generation of thoughtful and discerning thinkers ready to make an impact in the world of ideas. However, we also acknowledge the departure of 7 students, underscoring the importance of continued support and encouragement for our students throughout their journey.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
This month, we proudly celebrate the achievements of three exceptional graduates: Aisha Khan, Luis Gonzalez, and Ahmad El-Husseini. Each has demonstrated a profound understanding of philosophical inquiry, and we are confident that they will continue to make significant contributions to our field.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into July, we anticipate the arrival of new students, eager to join our community of critical thinkers. To ensure the success of these incoming students, we must remain committed to delivering high-quality education and fostering a supportive learning environment. We encourage all members of our school to continue striving for excellence in both their studies and interactions with one another. Let us carry forward the spirit of inquiry and intellectual rigor that defines our institution.
