This October marked a significant milestone for the London School of Critical Thinking, as we welcomed 16 new students and saw 61 graduates embark on their next critical thinking journeys. With 20 dropouts, it's clear that our rigorous curriculum is demanding but rewarding, fostering deep understanding in the field. The active student count of 9 leaves us poised for continued growth and success.
