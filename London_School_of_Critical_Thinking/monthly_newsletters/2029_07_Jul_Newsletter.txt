 MONTHLY SUMMARY:
July 2029 marked a vibrant and productive month at the London School of Critical Thinking. With an active student body of 20, we welcomed 78 graduates into the ranks of our alumni, while 22 students had to part ways due to varying circumstances. The energy and engagement in our classrooms were palpable, as evidenced by the capacity utilization, with all four cohorts maximizing our school's seating of 16. As we delve deeper into the realm of critical thinking, it is evident that our mission to cultivate a generation of thoughtful, analytical minds is well underway.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT:
This month, we are particularly proud of seven outstanding students who received Unit Awards in recognition of their exceptional accomplishments: Yara El-Sayed (Logic and Reasoning), Emma Patel, Anais Patel, Sophia Li, Zara Lee, Aisha Kumar, and Mohammad Al-Said (Philosophical Inquiry). Their dedication to critical thinking has truly made an impact in our academic community.

LOOKING AHEAD:
August lies ahead brimming with potential for continued growth and development. As we bid farewell to our graduating students, we look forward to welcoming a new cohort of inquisitive minds eager to explore the art and science of critical thinking. The challenges are numerous, but with resilience, dedication, and a shared commitment to intellectual rigor, we are confident that our community will continue to thrive. Let us embark on this exciting journey together!
