 MONTHLY SUMMARY
This September marked a significant milestone for the London School of Critical Thinking, with 84 graduates joining our growing network of critical thinkers. Despite the departure of 23 students and a slight decline in class size, we remain committed to nurturing the next generation of thinkers who will question, analyze, and shape their world. Our focus on the essential skills of logic, reasoning, and philosophical inquiry continues to be relevant as ever.

GRADUATE SPOTLIGHT
Congratulations to our nine graduates: Sara Dos Santos, Sofia Li, Omar El-Khateeb, Isabella Garcia, Lucas Ng, Lukas Müller, Hassan Ahmed, Zara Lee, and Yara El-Sayed. Each of them has demonstrated exceptional critical thinking abilities, and we are proud to see their success in our field.

LOOKING AHEAD
As we move into October, our focus will be on maintaining a balanced classroom environment that fosters continuous growth and learning. We invite prospective students to join us and contribute to our vibrant intellectual community. The future of critical thinking lies in the hands of those who dare to question, and we look forward to helping more individuals become fearless thinkers.
