────────────────────────────────────────

Q1: Which logical reasoning is used when a conclusion is drawn from specific observations to make broad generalizations?
  Deductive Reasoning
  Inductive Reasoning
  Abduction
Answer: Inductive Reasoning

Q2: What form of logical inference is used when a plausible explanation for a given observation or set of observations is formed?
  Deductive Reasoning
  Inductive Reasoning
  Abduction
Answer: Abduction
